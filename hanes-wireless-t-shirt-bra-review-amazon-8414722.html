I'm Stocking Up on My Go-To Bra for Comfort and Lift While It's on Sale

Publish date: 2024-07-10

I’ve been personally victimized by bras; from digging straps to stabbing underwire, I’ve experienced it all. I’m more of a bare-it-all or put-on-a-nipple-cover kind of girl — but on the occasion that I do, I only opt for wire-free styles. They feel like you have nothing on and offer the same lift and support as an underwire bra, minus all the poking and prodding. Plus, there’s a seamless finish under clothing, which feels like a second skin — I honestly forget I have one on. For this reason, my bra drawer is primarily made up of Hanes’ Wireless T-Shirt Bra, which I plan to stock up on while it’s 66 percent off at Amazon.

The T-shirt bra comes in eight colors and ranges from sizes 34A through 38D. It features a wire-free design, V-neckline that’s perfect for deep-plunging tops, adjustable slider shoulder straps that can be converted into a racerback, and a hook-and-eye back closure for easy on and off. The fabric is buttery smooth, super soft, moisture-wicking, while lightly lined foam cups conceal and support.

With holiday dinners, family gatherings, and more festivities underway this season, I need the extra concealment and seamlessness under my clothing.
