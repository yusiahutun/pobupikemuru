    By Beth BranchUpdated: Oct 4, 2023Save ArticleDana Gallagher

    If you're looking for the ultimate comfort food for your Thanksgiving menu, the South has everything you'd want. The simple, ingredient-forward recipes are as rich and delicious as they come.

    We've rounded up some of our absolute favorites, including Cajun recipes, Appalachian fare, the food ways of the Coastal South, and the Southwest, just to name a few. But no matter where you live or where you're from, add a little Southern flare to your menu this Thanksgiving!
