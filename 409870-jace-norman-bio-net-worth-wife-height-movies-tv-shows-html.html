Early life

Jace Lee Norman was born on March 21, 2000, in Corrales, New Mexico. He lived there with his family until he was eight years. Afterwards, the family moved to San Diego in Southern California, where he grew up alongside two older siblings.

Jace Norman had a pretty normal childhood at home. He spent time with his brother and sister in various outdoor activities. However, the school front was another different issue, at least for a while. After moving to Southern California, he enrolled in a public school. Here, he experienced several problems due to a condition he had.

What disease does Jace Norman have that affected his school life? He has dyslexia – a reading disability. As a result, he experienced difficulty reading, which led to poor grades in middle school. The condition, poor grades, and passion for acting made him a target for bullies.

Career

Jace Norman's passion for acting started when he was very young. Surprisingly, his brother was interested in the career, and Jace just tagged along. Soon, he had developed a love for it, and he and his brother were making and uploading YouTube videos. He had also grown up watching Disney, and Nickelodeon shows and always thought actors were cool.

When he informed his parents of his desires, they first pushed him into the direction of musical theatres. However, Norman's interests lay elsewhere – movies and TV shows, and more specifically, comedies.

When Norman was 12, his parents finally relented after seeing how passionate he was and got him an agent. On his first audition, he landed his first role as a guest actor on the Disney show Jessie.
