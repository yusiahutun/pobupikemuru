Obviously, snagging football-themed clothing that shows your support for the guys playing is a top priority, so if you’re wondering where to buy Chiefs jerseys, sweatshirts, and the like, you’ve come to the right place.

Unfortunately, Chiefs gear is quickly selling out everywhere, or worse, won’t ship in time for Sunday. The list ahead is packed with fun stuff, and you might feel tempted to get something for yourself and your pal who’s less into the Love Story and more into, you know, the sport itself.
