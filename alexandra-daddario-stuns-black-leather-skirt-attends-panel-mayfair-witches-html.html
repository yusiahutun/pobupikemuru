Mayfair Witches 'follows a neurosurgeon who discovers she is the unlikely heir to a family of witches. She must contend with a sinister presence that has haunted her family for generations,' according to the show's IMDb page. 

Daddario plays the aforementioned neurosurgeon. Hamlin plays her family's patriarch while Chirisa plays an agent from the enigmatic Talamasca organization.

The show airs on Hulu and with new episodes coming out every Sunday.
