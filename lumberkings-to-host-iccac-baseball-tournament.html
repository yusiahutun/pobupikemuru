LumberKings to host ICCAC baseball tournament

Publish date: 2024-06-06

by: Staff

Posted: May 6, 2019 / 09:11 PM CDT

Updated: May 6, 2019 / 09:11 PM CDT

The Clinton LumberKings will play host to the Division II ICCAC NJCAA Region XI baseball tournament for the second consecutive year, starting Wednesday and concluding May 13.  

The ICCAC, which operates the tournament, brings eight collegiate baseball teams into the Clinton area for the week-long competition.

The tournament features student-athletes from several Midwestern schools, including 2018 champion Kirkwood Community College.

Fans can enjoy up to three games each day.

Play gets started with the play-in game Wednesday Night featuring Iowa Lakes and Ellsworth.

First pitch is currently scheduled for 6 p.m.

A full tournament schedule can be seen above. Tickets begin at $5 for students and $10 for adults.
