Emily Ratajkowski wears a buttery yellow jacket

Publish date: 2024-05-10

A buttery yellow can often evoke images of bright little flowers sprouting up in your yard or a creamy pastry purchased on a warm midday walk, so it’s no wonder the tint has become the colour of spring.

If you weren’t convinced by Selena Gomez showing off her butter-yellow mani on Instagram, Aubrey Plaza wearing the colour to the Emmys, or Sandy Liang dressing models in the creamy colour for her autumn/winter 2024 collection, now Emily Ratajkowski is embracing the hue as well.

Gotham//Getty Images

Related Story

Out and about in New York City on Monday, the model was seen touting the trending tone with her lightweight faux-leather jacket, which came in a soft yellow and featured a zipper enclosure and two chest pockets. However, Ratajkowski left the outerwear hanging open to reveal a black long-sleeved crop top underneath, which she paired with tawny slacks that hugged her legs and pooled around her black mules. She also accessorised with a pair of black oval sunglasses, golden square earrings, and a black leather shoulder bag by Loewe.

The appearance of buttery yellow in Ratajkowski’s ensemble was a fresh surprise, given that she’s been opting for more earthy browns and greens with her recent ensembles. Just a little over a week ago, Ratajkowski was spotted strolling through New York in a parchment-coloured jacket, which she buttoned over a black hoodie and paired with pistachio-green trousers and Puma sneakers. A few weeks before that, she took her dog, Colombo, for a walk and wore a fuzzy caramel coat over a grey tracksuit.
