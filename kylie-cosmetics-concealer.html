Kylie Cosmetics Concealers - Kylie Jenner Concealers 30 Shades

Publish date: 2024-03-04

The long-teased Kylie Cosmetics Skin Concealers are finally here. Well, almost. The brand just teased on Instagram that 30 (!!!) shades of liquid concealer will be released on December 13, the same day as the new bullet lipsticks.

These concealers have been in the works for a long time. The formula claims to be long-lasting and ideal for all skin types, but it appears that the shade range is what Kylie Jenner really honed in on—at least at first glance. The 30 shades cover almost every tone on the spectrum in her swatches. See the lightest pale shade next to the darkest shade, below.

Kylie Cosmetics

"I wear these every single day," Jenner says on Snapchat. "This is a medium coverage that's super buildable." She swatched all the shades, then showed off her own personal tube in the color Maple. "I picked this paddle because it adds the perfect amount of product and you only have to dip it once in the bottle," she says about the doe-foot applicator.

Consider this a warning to Tarte Shape Tape and Nars Radiant Cream Concealer—there could be a new formula coming for your crown. Actually, two new formulas. Kim Kardashian West confirmed to BAZAAR.com this month that she will be releasing concealers in 2018 under her KKW Beauty label. "Concealers—I was going to launch concealers next, but I really wanted to perfect it. So I think concealers will be next," she told BAZAAR.com.
