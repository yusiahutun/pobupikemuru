So Exciting!Josh Hartnett and Tamsin Egerton Are Expecting Baby No. 2

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Congratulations are in order!

Josh Hartnett, 38, and his longtime girlfriend, Tamsin Egerton, are expecting their second baby together! The 28-year-old model flaunted her growing belly for the first time at the Vanity Fair Oscars party on Sunday, Feb. 26.

At the event, pregnant Tamsin happily enjoyed some fried dough. “This is not feeding the baby nourishment,” she joked, according to People.

Tamsin showing off her baby bump alongside Josh.

Josh and Tamsin first began dating in 2012 and welcomed a daughter (whose name has not yet been revealed) in late 2015.

The Pearl Harbor star has opened up in the past about his choice to keep his personal life very private. “I purposefully didn’t want people to know too much about my personal life because I think a lot of people give it away for free,” the revealed in a previous interview with USA Today. “I think if you spend too much time courting people’s attention into your personal life, you lose your ownership of it.”

However, that hasn’t stopped him from raving about life as a dad in the past! “She’s the apple of my eye, she’s perfect in every way,” he told Kelly Ripa and Michael Strahan on Live! of his then five-month-old little girl.
