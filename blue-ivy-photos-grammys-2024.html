Blue Ivy Looks So Pretty in a Cinderella Gown at the 2024 Grammys

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Blue Ivy looked like red carpet royalty at the 66th Grammy Awards ceremony.

Sneaking into the Crypto.com Arena with parents Jay-Z and Beyoncé long after the red carpet wrapped Sunday night, the 12-year-old accompanied her dad onstage as he accepted a black gramophone for the Dr. Dre Global Impact Award.

Blue looked radiant in a luminescent white midi dress—a custom Vivienne Westwood Couture design—which featured an off-the-shoulder neckline, a corseted bodice, and a puffy skirt with asymmetrical draping details. She paired the dress with white platform boots from Larroudé, diamond-encrusted jewelry from Boucheron, and a matching clutch bag by Jeffrey Levinson.

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

While beginning his acceptance speech, Jay-Z jokingly referred to the trophy as a “sippy cup” for Blue, though he quickly amended his statement: “Blue’s grown now. She doesn’t take sippy cups—and she has her own Grammys.”

In case you forgot, Blue became the second-youngest Grammy winner ever in 2021, when she and Beyoncé won the Best Music Video award for “Brown Skin Girl.”

VALERIE MACON//Getty Images

In 2020, Bey reflected on the sentimental significance of the track, which premiered as part of visual album Black Is King on Disney+.

“It was so important to me in ‘Brown Skin Girl’ that we represented all different shades of brown, and I wanted every character to be shot in a regal light,” she explained to Good Morning America. “It was important that we’re all in this together and we all are celebrating each other.”

The video also features cameos from Blue’s grandmother Tina Knowles-Lawson and younger sister, Rumi.
