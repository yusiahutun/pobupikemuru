Nigella Lawson mocked after making meal which 'looks like naked women'

Publish date: 2024-03-26

Nigella Lawson has been mocked after creating a dish that looks like naked women lying on their backs.

The telly kitchen queen left saucy fans giggling when she posted a photo of her Hoisin Quail dish on social media.

The photo saw a row of three pieces of meat laid across a tray, but fans couldn't help but think it looked like women with their legs pushed together and a busty chest on their backs.

Fans were up in arms at the photo and immediately swarmed to the comment section to share their thoughts on the unusual looking dish, with one user writing: "At first glance, I thought this was a woman lying on her back with her knees up."

Another added: "Sunbathing ladies with burnt knees and toes."

And a third told the 62-year-old cook: “Looks like me on a sun lounger.”

But Nigella said: "I love these sweet and meaty quail, conker-shiny in their vaguely Chinesey marinade.

"The thing is to marinate them for a good long time, at least 24 hours, so that they keep fleshy and juicy and meltingly tender no matter how brutally you cook them."

It’s not the first time “Domestic Goddess” Nigella has left food lovers baffled with her bizarre concoctions.

In June, she confessed to having mixed Marmite in with spaghetti.

And in January she was mocked for sharing a super-rare Aberdeen Angus tomahawk steak which some of her critics claimed “was still mooing”.

Last year, she cooked potatoes at Christmas which had been covered in caramel.

Nigella has even cooked rump steak with vodka and put bacon in chocolate brownies.

She also left fans of her telly shows scratching their heads by pronouncing “microwave” as “mee-cro-wah-vay”.

While she's certainly drummed up controversy around her meals, one thing many fans can agree on is that she comes up with plenty of incredible recipes - and she shares a chunk of them online.
