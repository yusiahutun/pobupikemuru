Astrology lovers, rejoice: Van Cleef & Arpels looks to the stars for new timepiece collection

Van Cleef & Arpels is launching the Lady Arpels Zodiac Lumineux timepieces as well as new jewellery items under its sparkling Snowflake collection.

The new series includes 12 feminine zodiac timepieces. It joins the masculine Midnight Zodiac Lumineux watch collection that the brand introduced earlier this year at Salon International de la Haute Horlogerie, with both depicting the beauty of illuminating constellations and nature.

For example, the dial of the Capricorn watch features yellow gold, glittering blue enamel, blue sapphire, translucent green enamel and diamonds.

The Snowflake collection incorporates four new diamond-encrusted pieces that resemble a snow-covered landscape. The new items include two clips and two transformable necklaces, all set with white gold, diamonds and platinum.
