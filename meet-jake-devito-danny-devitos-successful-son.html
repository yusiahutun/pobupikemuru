Meet Jake DeVito, Danny Devito's Successful Son

Publish date: 2024-02-05

Danny DeVito wears many hats, but his favorite is the one that pronounces him father, husband, and friend. Danny is the proud father of three kids he shares with his former wife, Rhea Perlman. Jake DeVito is one of the actor’s children and their only son. 

His sisters are named Lucy and Grace. Danny has praised all three in the past for keeping their heads together despite the fame and success they were born into. 

Here is everything we know about Jake DeVito. 

Jake DeVito Bio

Jacob “Jake” Daniel DeVito was born in New York on October 10, 1987. His parents are Danny DeVito and his ex-wife Rhea Perlman. He is of Italian descent, but since he was born in New York, he holds American citizenship and is a Christian. 

Who Is Danny DeVito’s Son? 

Danny DeVito’s son is named Jake DeVito and, like his father, also chose to pursue a career in showbiz. Jake was born to Danny and Perlman in October 1987. Given his father’s fame, Jake was famous even before he was born. 

However, Jake and his sisters remained grounded despite their family’s fame. Danny said in a 2022 interview: 

“They were perfect about keeping our home solid and safe. I always feel like we always were more just excited about being at home with our friends and in our community.” 

Even though Jake works in the entertainment industry, most of his work is done behind the camera. He has worked as a producer on several projects, including “The Better Angels” and an upcoming thriller titled “St. Sebastian.” 

One of Jake’s most notable projects has been working alongside his father and sister as an executive producer on the “Little Demon” animated series. 

Jake DeVito’s Family

Jake’s parents were married for over three decades. The couple met on January 17, 1971, during a theatrical play, “The Shrinking Bride.” 

Perlman had been at the play to support her friend, who was one of the actors, while Danny had been a part of the cast. They caught each other’s eyes, and a romantic relationship blossomed afterward. 

They acted for two weeks before they decided to start living together. Then on January 28, 1982, they got married. Danny and Perlman welcomed their first child, Jake’s eldest sister, Lucy Chet DeVito, in 1982. She is an actress with credits on TV and the big screen.  

Danny and Perlman welcomed their second daughter and child in 1985. She is the most private among the three and is also an actress. 

 Jake DeVito Movies 

Since he launched his career, Jake DeVito has shown the world his talents as an actor and a movie producer. Some of the movies he starred in and others he was part of the production team are listed below. 

As a producer, Jake DeVito has worked on the following projects: 

  • Little Demon (TV Series, 2022) – Executive Producer, ten episodes
  • Wererock (Short, 2020) – Producer
  • Curmudgeons (Short, 2016) – Producer
  • Fair Chase (Film, 2014) – Associate Producer
  • Quicksand (Short, 2014) – Producer
  • The Better Angels (Film, 2014) – Producer

As an actor, Jake DeVito has starred in the following projects: “Pieces of a Dream” (2005) as David Lekkas, “Amboy” (2015), “Curmudgeons” (Short, 2016), and “Untitled Tim Long Project.”

Jake DeVito’s Net Worth

According to some sources, Jake DeVito is worth an estimated $1 million. Most of his money came from his work as an actor and producer. He has also made money through sponsored commercials, brand promotions, and other revenue streams.

Jake DeVito Instagram

Is Jake DeVito Married? 

Jake is unmarried, and because he cherishes his privacy, his personal matters are rarely made public. 

Jake DeVito Age

Danny DeVito’s son, Jake, is 35 years old at this time. The talented producer will clock 36 in October 2023. Other stars joining him to celebrate their birthdays in the same month include Katy Perry, Kim Kardashian, and Matt Damon.
