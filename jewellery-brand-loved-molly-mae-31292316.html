Jewellery brand loved by Molly-Mae launches clover necklace similar to Queen Camilla's

Publish date: 2024-05-18

When Molly-Mae Hague attended her fiancé Tommy Fury's fight in Manchester, fans were crazy about her gold "Tommy" necklace.

Abbott Lyon, the brand that created the jewellery piece, went viral and the personalised pieces are flying off the shelves. Fans said they "need" to get one and some raced to buy one for themsevles and their friends.

The boutique brand has since launched a new personalised necklace in pretty clover tabs in three beautiful styles — molten, textured and pearl effect.

The dainty clover tabs can be customised with up to eight initials on a gold plated stainless steel chain or a silver stinless steel chain. With prices starting from £75, fans are excited to get multiple versions.

In one post on Abbott Lyon's Instagram, they show a model layering two necklaces together while showcasing the molten finish charms and the mother of pearl texture.

A caption read: "Necklaces that will make you look expensive."

Safe to wear at the gym and in swimming pool, the head-turning piece is also heat-resistant and is complete with anti-tarnish coating.

The Clover Custom Neclace takes inspirations from luxury brand Van Cleef & Arpels. The pieces in their Alhambra necklace collection start from £1,430.

Royal fans might have already known but Queen Camilla is a big fan of the Alhambra collection. She was seen sporting a £4,500 Van Cleef & Arpels bracelet when she attended Sandringham Flower Show with her husband King Charles in July.

In 2017, the Queen Consort was snapped wearing a green Alhambra necklace with matching earrings when attending at charity launch.

One shopper said: "Ordered mine, a birthday gift to me, love from me."

Another wrote: "This is beautiful I absolutely need this."

A third tagged her friend and added: "These are so pretty. We need one each."

If you are a royal fan, you might also be interested in Welsh jewellery brand Clogau. All their pieces contain rare Welsh gold — the very same gold used to craft many of the Royal Family's wedding rings.

They launched a "12 days of Clogau" jewellery advent calendar for Christmas and shoppers can get over £880 worth of accessories and handbag essentials.

For gem lovers, Fraser Hart have some brilliant lab-grown diamond pieces available with a more accessible price tag. The Born Lab Grown Diamond collection delivers the same sparkling wow-factor as traditionally mined gems.

With exquisite diamond rings starting at just £799, diamond and white gold stud earrings from £799, shoppers are given a wide choice of cuts, carat sizes and settings, giving customers the freedom to find the ultimate diamond jewellery piece to cherish forever.
