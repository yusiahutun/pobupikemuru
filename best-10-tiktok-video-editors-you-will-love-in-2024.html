Best 10 TikTok Video Editors You Will Love in 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-22

One of the key factors to succeed on TikTok is the video quality, storytelling and the editing behind it. Therefore, having the right tool would put you one step ahead if your goal is to gain some popularity in this video platform. With the right TikTok editor, you can transform your raw footage into captivating.

How to Edit TikTok Videos: Top 4 Tips

Cut your videos

TikTok supports videos up to 10 minutes long, but shorter videos tend to generate higher engagement. Therefore, cut down your videos to the most engaging parts. Remove any dead air, repetition, or anything else that could distract the viewer. This will also make it easier to create smooth transitions between clips.

Use filters

Filters are widely used on TikTok as they can give your videos a unique look. Play around with different filters to find the one that best suits your content. Some popular TikTok filters are the ‘Beauty’ filter, ‘Face Zoom’ effect, ‘Anime’ filter, ‘G6’ filter, and others.

Add music & sound effects

Music is the most important element on TikTok as it’s what makes people watch, rewatch, try new trends, and share. Choose a catchy, popular song or sound effect that fits your content and helps convey the desired emotion. You can search from thousands of music tracks and sounds available in TikTok’s library or even make your own one.

Include text & stickers

Since most mobile users watch videos with no sound, adding captions is a crucial factor to engage your viewers. Try including a few relevant hashtags in your captions to draw even more attention. Moreover, like on Instagram Stories, you can add stickers to your TikTok videos to add a fun element and evoke emotions or reactions.

Top 10 TikTok Video Editors to Level Up Your Content

CapCut, owned by ByteDance, the creators of TikTok, is an easy-to-use video editor for TikTok. It allows you to split, reverse, trim, speed up, and rotate videos quickly. Users can add your own audio, text, fonts, filters, effects, and transitions. You can also layer multiple videos to create complex visual stories.

Pros

  • User-friendly interface
  • Plenty of tools and templates
  • Creative effects and filters
  • Built-in music and sound effects library
  • Easy sharing options

Cons

  • Limited advanced features compared to professional editing software
  • Some features may require a subscription

Pricing

CapCut is completely free. However, to access premium features and more cloud storage, users have to purchase CapCut Pro for $7.99/month or as a one-time payment of $9.99

Compatibility: PC, iOS, Android

  • Movavi Video Editor

If you need a simple but feature-rich TikTok editor, Movavi Video Editor makes video editing a breeze. It offers both basic and advanced video and audio editing tools to suit any type of user.

Movavi Video Editor also has some automated features like short movie creation, color correction, background removal, noise reduction, and more.

Pricing
