Henry Aronofsky: What You Need to About Rachel Weiszs Son

Publish date: 2024-02-18

Henry Aronofsky is the son of talented actress Rachel Weisz and acclaimed filmmaker Darren Aronofsky. He is also the stepson of the skilled actor Daniel Craig. 

Surrounded by such talent, many can’t help but wonder about the upbringing and experiences Henry has been exposed to. Also, they cannot overlook the possibility of what he can achieve should he pursue an entertainment career.

It is still being determined if Henry will follow in their footsteps or if he will chart a new course for himself outside Hollywood, where his parents made their fame and fortune. Keep reading to learn about him and his family. 

Who Is the Father of Henry Aronofsky?

Henry Aronofsky’s father is the famous filmmaker Darren Aronofsky, and his mother is the talented actress Rachel Weisz. Darren is renowned for directing critically acclaimed films like “Pi,” “Requiem for a Dream,” and ‘”The Wrestler,” while Weisz is best known for her portrayal of Evelyn Carnahan in “The Mummy.”

The former couple welcomed him in New York City, New York, on May 31, 2006. Henry’s parents, who were engaged at his birth, went their separate ways four years later. However, they were able to reach an agreement and have remained cordial towards the goal of co-parenting. 

His parents work hard to keep him from the spotlight, but this isn’t easy simply because of his links to them and a veteran like Daniel Craig. Henry Aronofsky reportedly lives in Brooklyn, New York, with his mother, with whom he shares a close relationship. 

He hardly ever makes public appearances but is known to spend a lot of time together with his mom. The pair have been spotted strolling around the streets of London and New York side by side several times. 

Since he broke off his engagement from Weisz, Darren has not been as open about his romantic life. However, we know that in September 2016, he and actress Jennifer Lawrence were revealed to be in a relationship. The relationship lasted one year before it ended in November 2017 due to unknown causes. 

Who Is Henry Aronofsky’s Stepfather? 

Henry Aronofsky’s stepfather is Daniel Craig. The “James Bond” actor married Henry’s mother in an intimate wedding witnessed by four people in 2011. Henry and Craig are believed to get along well; the young boy has been seen with the actor in public several times, usually in the company of his mother. 

In 2012, he was seen with Craig and Weisz when they had dinner in New York City. He was pictured again in December 2016 when he joined his mother and Craig’s friend Mark Strong as the trio supported Craig, who played an important role in Shakespeare’s “Othello” in a theater production in New York City.

Henry Aronofsky Age

Henry’s current age, as of 2023, is 17. He was born on May 31, 2006, and is expected to celebrate his 18th birthday in 2024.

Henry Aronofsky’s Net Worth

Henry Aronofsky’s net worth has not been determined at the time of this writing. This is because he has no known source of income and is not linked to any financial endeavors. Nevertheless, it is believed Henry lives a luxurious life thanks mainly to his parents, who have acquired stunning net worths. 

His mother is estimated to be worth $40 million as of 2023, while his biological father reportedly controls a fortune of $25 million. His stepfather, Daniel Craig’s net worth dwarfs them both as it is estimated to be $160 million. 

Henry Aronofsky Family

He is descended from a large and talented family. His paternal grandfather, Abraham Aronofsky, is a retired chemistry teacher and an actor, best known for his roles in films like “The Fountain” (2006), “Pi (1998), and “Black Swan” (2010). 

Henry’s paternal grandmother, Charlotte Aronofsky, is also an actress known to have appeared in movies like “Requiem for a Dream,” “Black Swan” and “Pi.” His maternal grandfather, George Weisz, was not an entertainer but held down relevant roles in society as a mechanical engineer and inventor.

His wife, Edith Ruth, is a teacher and therapist. Henry got a sibling in September 2018 when Rachel Weisz and Daniel Craig welcomed a daughter. 

He also gets along well with his step-sister, Ella Craig, born in 1992. Ella is the product of her father’s first marriage to model Fiona Loudon.
