See Kendall Jenner Look Parisian Chic in a Gray Sweater Dress and Stiletto Boots

Publish date: 2024-03-19

Kendall Jenner's trip to Paris is delivering one sophisticated look after another.

Yesterday, the model continued her international getaway with another chic outing. She was photographed out and about in the French capital, where she wore an elegant and cozy spin on her typical sleek street style.

The reality star's ensemble was comprised of a chic gray wool dress by Ann Demeulemeester, featuring long sleeves, an asymmetrical thigh-high slit, and a mock neck with an attached full-length scarf adornment over the right shoulder. She styled the piece with knee-high pointed-toe leather boots by The Row, and accessories including a black leather tote and oval sunglasses. As for her glam look, the star pulled her hair back in a middle-part ponytail wore with a nude lip.

BACKGRID

Earlier this week, the 818 founder channeled Audrey Hepburn in an Instagram dispatch from her Paris trip. In the pics, the model lounges on a cream-and-gold couch in an elaborate floral pattern, inside a romantically adorned suite.

For her vintage-inspired look, the star wore a silky scoop-neck black top tucked into a matching midi skirt and paired with a black headscarf, which she wrapped around her neck and let hang in the back. She accessorized the minimalistic ensemble with black pointed-toe leather kitten heels, and black elongated sunglasses, while her makeup looo included subtle pink blush and a rosy nude lip.

She captioned the post with a simple croissant emoji.

Jenner is known for her sophisticated street style. Earlier this month, she was seen out in Beverly Hills wearing an elevated off-duty look comprised of a shiny light brown trench coat with gold buttons from FWRD, a simple white tee from Leset, a beige maxi skirt with a high leg slit, and black leather knee-high boots with bent stiletto heels. She also looked chic during a February outing in Milan, where she wore a sleek all-black business look broken up by a cream shearling jacket with ivory fur detailing.
