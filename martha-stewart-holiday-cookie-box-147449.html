Martha Stewart's First Ever Holiday Cookie Baking Kit with Marley Spoon

Publish date: 2024-03-27

We’ve got big news for Martha Stewart fans, bonafide bakers, and baking enthusiasts: Today marks Marley Spoon’s official release of Martha’s Holiday Cookie Box. Don’t worry; it’s not as intimidating as it sounds. The kit is designed for skilled and novice bakers alike and includes pre-portioned, dry ingredients and easy-to-follow, step-by-step recipe cards for four of Martha’s tastiest sweet treats.

Each kit will also include parchment cookie bags and red-and-white baker’s twine, so you’ll be ready to present your freshly-baked goods in style, whether you’re baking for a PTA meeting, a church fundraiser, or your loved ones.

Martha’s Holiday Cookie Box Recipes

Martha’s Holiday Cookie Box, which yields 11 dozen cookies, includes recipes and ingredients for Big Martha’s Noel Nut Balls, Brown Butter Cardamom Cookies, Chewy Chocolate-Gingerbread Cookies, and Double-Chocolate Peppermint Brownies.

“This special new box allows you to do all of your own holiday baking in a simple and easy way,” Stewart said in a statement. “We provide you with everything you need from pre-measured ingredients to gift giving packaging options. The recipes are delicious and are sure to fill your home with the sweet, pleasurable aroma.”

Big Martha’s Noel Nut Balls

(Photo Credit: Martha & Marley Spoon)

Big Martha’s Noel Nut Balls recipe is a recipe that’s been in the family for generations. Made with honey and freshly chopped pecans, these sweet bite-sized balls are sure to be a hit at your next get-together.

Butter Brown Cardamom Cookies

(Photo Credit: Martha & Marley Spoon)

Brown butter and pure vanilla extract combine to create this decadent cookie. Dusted with a little colored sanding sugar to give it a festive holiday look, Martha’s Brown Butter Cardamom Cookies are the perfect finish to any holiday meal.

Chewy Chocolate-Gingerbread Cookies

(Photo Credit: Martha & Marley Spoon)

Martha’s Chewy Chocolate-Gingerbread Cookies have just the right amount of bittersweet chocolate and molasses to make one very delicious holiday cookie.

Double-Chocolate Peppermint Brownies

(Photo Credit: Martha & Marley Spoon)

We’ve saved (possibly the best recipe in this collection) for last. The perfect mix of peppermint, bittersweet chocolate, and cocoa powder will make these fudgy brownies your new holiday go-to.

(Photo Credit: Martha & Marley Spoon)

Get Martha’s Holiday Cookie Box for just $38.99 at marleyspoon.com now through January 3, 2018.
