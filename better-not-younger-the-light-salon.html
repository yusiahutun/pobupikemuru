Better Not Younger: The Light Salon

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Picture: The Light Salon at Harvey Nichols

Eleven minutes to achieve rejuvenated skin may sound like an impossible feat, but thanks to the latest in LED light technology, it's now within reach.

Step into the chic seclusion of the Light Salon in Harvey Nichols, sink into a lounge chair and simply bask under a lamp of soothingly warm near-infra-red light (the opposite of damaging UV). The results within just one treatment were impressive: my skin immediately looked decidedly more even and radiant.

The real benefits are cumulative, with 10 treatments over a fortnight being optimal: penetrating deeper than creams or serums, the light spectrum boosts levels of collagen, elastin and hyalauronic acid, resulting in a complexion that is smoother, tighter and more plump.

Many 'lunch time' facial treatments underwhelm; this innovative but science-based concept doesn't. For a fast but incredibly effective and relaxing treatment, look no further.

The Light Salon at Harvey Nichols, from £35 a treatment, www.thelight-salon.com
