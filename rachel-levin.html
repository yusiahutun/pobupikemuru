Biography Timeline

Rachel Claire Levin, also known as Rclbeauty101, is a famous American YouTuber and social media personality. She is known for her beauty and fashion-related content, as well as her lifestyle and comedy videos. This biography timeline provides an overview of her life and accomplishments.

  • February 24, 1995: Rachel Levin was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 2010: Levin created her YouTube channel, Rclbeauty101, and uploaded beauty and fashion-related videos.
  • 2013: She gained significant popularity for her video “How to Trick People Into Thinking You’re Good Looking,” which has over 70 million views.
  • 2015: Levin won the award for Breakout Creator at the Streamy Awards.
  • 2016: She released her first book, “My Life as Eva: The Struggle is Real,” which was a New York Times bestseller.
  • 2017: Levin was named as one of Forbes’ 30 Under 30 in the category of Art & Style.
  • 2018: She starred in the YouTube original series “Me and My Grandma” alongside actress Rhea Perlman.
  • 2019: Levin launched her makeup brand, “BoxyCharm x Rclbeauty101.”
  • 2020: She released her second book, “You’re Not Special: A (Sort-of) Memoir.”
  • 2020: Levin served as a judge on the reality competition show “Instant Influencer” hosted by James Charles.
  • 2021: She launched her podcast, “The Real Reel with Rachel,” where she interviews guests on topics ranging from mental health to pop culture.

Rachel Levin has built a successful career through her YouTube channel and social media presence, with over 14 million subscribers on YouTube and over 3 million followers on Instagram. Her relatable personality, humour, and passion for creativity have earned her a dedicated fanbase and recognition within the entertainment industry. She continues to inspire and entertain her followers through various endeavours and is a role model for aspiring content creators.
