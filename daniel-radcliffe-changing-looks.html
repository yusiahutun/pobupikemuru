Daniel Radcliffe's Changing Looks

Publish date: 2024-04-16

Watch the Harry Potter actor transform from an adorable child star to a handsome young man.

2000

The 11-year-old actor looked adorable in Harry's signature round glasses at a press conference for the first film in the franchise, Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone.

2001

Radcliffe matched his shirt to his baby blues at the world premiere of the first Harry Potter film in London.

2004

Three years later, the star appeared on the red carpet for the third film, Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, in a pinstriped suit.

2005

Radcliffe looked far more grown up at the Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire Paris premiere, forgoing his shaggy locks for a shorter 'do.

2008

The 19-year-old became a bonafide heartthrob, showing off facial scruff, a trench coat, and black tee.

2009

A clean-shaven Radcliffe showed off his fashion sense in a gray suit with black piping at a Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince premiere.

2010

The 21-year-old actor beamed at the N.Y.C. premiere of Deathly Hallows: Part 1, showing off a longer, shaggy cut.

2011

What a gentleman! Radcliffe looked spiffy in a tux and coiffed 'do at the opening night of his Broadway play, How to Succeed in Business Without Really Trying.

2012

The actor combed back his long locks and showed off some groomed facial hair at a screening of The Woman in Black.

2013

Radcliffe chopped off his wavy locks after growing them out for the 2013 period drama, Kill Your Darlings.

2015

Bald and beautiful! Radcliffe showed off a shaved head at the New York premiere of Victor Frankenstein.

2016

One year later, his lush locks are back—along with new facial hair—at the premiere of Now You See Me 2, in which his character performs a very different kind of magic.
