Hala Sultan Tekke, also known as the Mosque of Umm Haram, is located in Larnaca, Cyprus. It holds immense historical and religious significance for Muslims around the world.

Hala Sultan Tekke is considered one of the holiest Islamic sites outside of Arabia. It is the final resting place of Umm Haram, a beloved companion of the Prophet Muhammad. Her tomb is housed within the mosque, making it a popular pilgrimage site for Muslims.

The mosque’s architectural design is a blend of Ottoman and Byzantine influences. Its iconic white marble dome and minaret stand tall against the clear blue sky, creating a picturesque sight.
