10 Best Pumping Bras 2024, Tested &amp; Reviewed

Publish date: 2024-07-02

Without question, this is the best pumping bra I've worn. Pre-baby, I rarely wore a bra, and if I did, it was an airy bralette sans hooks or wires—so pumping was a real lifestyle change for me. The fabric is incredibly silky and supportive without feeling restrictive, plus the simplified, non-clip-down design makes my quality of life much better while pumping. I've been rotating through several for months and rarely wear a regular bra since I nurse and pump daily. I sleep in mine, too!

"We wanted a design that enables you to pump hands-free while wearing the same bra all day, every day," Haim says of her design. "The Do Anything Bra was developed with an IBCLC to optimize breast health, crafted with premium OEKO-TEX fabric that's gentle on sensitive skin, and designed with highly adjustable features (including a custom, five-row extended back closure, straps that convert to racerback and strap sliders positioned in the front) so you can comfortably wear it through all your bodily changes."

Sizes: S–3X

What reviewers are saying: "This bra has been perfect for breastfeeding and pumping. I am not exclusively nursing my baby, and this bra gives me the flexibility to nurse and pump throughout the day.
