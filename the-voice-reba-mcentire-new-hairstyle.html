'The Voice' Fans Are Going Wild Over Reba McEntire's Sleek Hair

Publish date: 2024-02-18

The Voice coach Reba McEntire has had many styles over the years, and for the famous redhead, it's usually the bigger, the better when it comes to her hair. Now, the 68-year-old singer is showing off a sleek new 'do, and fans can hardly handle it.

Reba is currently serving as a coach on season 24 of The Voice. She also is releasing her new book, Not That Fancy: Simple Lessons on Living, Loving, Eating and Dusting Off Your Boots, this week with a companion acoustic album.

If that's not enough, the three-time Grammy winner is celebrating a huge milestone: Her 1990 song "Fancy" has reached 100 million streams on Spotify. To celebrate, she put out a call for fans to post videos singing the iconic song, and Reba said she "just might duet or share your post!"

That happened to one lucky singer, who had the honor of singing alongside Reba through the magic of Tik Tok. The rendition has gotten more than 2.5 million views and 4,000 comments, many praised the singing, but a lot of fans took the time to comment on Reba's hair.

Reba noticeably swapped her coiffed hair for a very chic fringe, complete with sleek bangs and face-framing layers. Fans left comments like:

  • "REBA knows she is looking amazing!"
  • "Love your hair like that"
  • "That look on you! I didn't think you could be more gorgeous! Inside always. You're slaying outside!"
  • "I love your hair style, so pretty!!"
  • "I swear Reba only gets more beautiful every year!"
  • "Loving the new hair Reba!"

Catch Reba on The Voice when it airs on NBC on Monday and Tuesday nights, or stream episodes on Peacock.
