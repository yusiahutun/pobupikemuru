Kinder Bueno white chocolate and coconut bars make a return to UK stores from today

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Well we all love a Kinder... egg, white, minis, hazelnut, and at last the long awaited return of the Kinder Bueno white chocolate and coconut.

The tasty treat makes a return to our supermarket shelves today, so sweet-toothed chocolate lovers will finally be able to get their hands on the nutty tropical flavoured chocolate bar, once again.

The white chocolate and coconut bar is made from white chocolate that coats the Kinder Bueno wafer, which is filled with delicious hazelnut cream, giving us all beachy summer vibes. As if all that isn't enough, it's then topped with coconut flakes, for that extra tropical taste.

Snack lovers were able to get their hands on the nutty tropical flavoured chocolate bar until 2017, and for the first time in four years, they are available to buy in UK supermarkets such as Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda and convenience stores, at a cost of 60p.

Fans of the chocolate makers took to Instagram to comment on the brands page after they had posted about the return of the delicious coconut flavour.

Instagram

One fan wrote "Cannot wait for this" while another tagged in a friend saying "You'll be buzzing."

A third fan went on to simply say, "Yum" alongside a heart emoji and a fourth fan added, "YES YES YES."

The chocolate makers said at the time of the product's initial launch, that they were "looking to bring a bit of difference to shoppers and this time it's with a touch of tropical; perfectly timed for the busy summer season ahead for retailers.

"With adults making up two-thirds of Kinder Bueno consumption, we continuously strive to create confectionery that appeals to this age group.

"With the ingredient seeing a rise in consumer awareness in the last year, we're anticipating a strong performance for this exciting new variant."

Kinder Bueno has launched many deliciously exciting chocolate treats - Food Instagrammer @johnssnackreviews also recently spotted Kinder's Choco Fresh, on sale in Lidl stores for £1.99.

These mouth-watering chocolates boast a hazelnut cream that just dissolves on your tongue - we're already salivating.

You can also buy Kinder Bueno treats by the box load at Amazon.co.uk from £8.95, where you can choose one of their selection boxes to sample all that Kinder has to offer.
