TEEN Mom alum Kailyn Lowry has shown off her “bump” in new unedited photos, as fans suspect the “mom of five” is now pregnant with twins. 

Fans have speculated Kailyn, 31, is pregnant with twins just months after The U.S. Sun exclusively reported she secretly gave birth to her first child with her boyfriend, Elijah Scott, on November 20, 2022. 

Now, The U.S. Sun has obtained exclusive photos of the “mom of five” running errands in Smyrna, Delaware with an apparent bump. 

In the pictures taken on May 24, 2023, Kailyn attended her 5-year-old son Lux’s baseball game. 

Kailyn wore a black shirt and matching leggings, as her stomach bump could be seen in the photos.

She sported a serious expression throughout the game while watching her son play.

Fans have been speculating Kailyn is pregnant with twins in recent weeks.

PREGNANCY CLUES

The Pennsylvania native appeared in an Instagram Story ad earlier this month promoting Hiya Health vitamins.

She spoke directly into the camera as she discussed the benefits of the vitamins to her followers.

Throughout the entire video, the young mom flashed a colorful bracelet on her left wrist.

Eagle-eyed fans zoomed in on the beaded jewelry and noticed it said: "Mom of seven."

FAN CHATTER

A fan-run Instagram account called @TM_chatter reposted screenshots from the video on their profile, writing: "Confirmed. #KailLowry is pregnant with twins. She will be the mom of 7. You’re welcome."

Teen Mom fans appeared stunned by the idea and flocked to the account to share their own theories.

One wrote: "Just went and watched the stories myself and you’re right, it definitely does say 'mom of seven' lol. She tried to cover it with the text but I feel like that kinda just made it more obvious like pointed out to us that there was something to look for underneath!"

A second wrote: "Her face changes so much when she’s pregnant. It’s an instant tell."

KAIL RESPONDS

Kailyn seemingly responded to the rumors in a TikTok video with Elijah. 

In the six-second clip, Kailyn lip-synced to a quote about rumors.

"Ever heard a rumor about yourself and actually wanted to hear more? Like, 'damn, what did I do next?'" Kailyn mouthed to Elijah with a huge grin on her face.

Kailyn is already mom to Isaac, 13, who Kailyn shares with ex-boyfriend Jo Rivera, Lincoln, 9, with ex-husband Javi Marroquin, and Lux and Creed, 2, with ex Chris Lopez. 

Her son Rio with her boyfriend Elijah was born in November, a source previously claimed to The U.S. Sun.

KAIL'S DENIALS

The podcast host has denied the pregnancy rumors and has yet to introduce her fifth son.

The Pennsylvania native was quick to shut the conversation down, as she revealed: "I’m getting my tubes tied. I’m getting my tubes tied next month so that's gonna be a no.”

She also responded to fans questioning her heavy breathing, saying: "I'm literally obese."
