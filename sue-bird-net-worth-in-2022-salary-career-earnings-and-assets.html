Sue Bird's Parents And Ethnicity

Sue Bird was born Suzanne Brigit Bird to Herschel and Nancy Bird in Syosset, New York, on October 16, 1980. She has an elder sister, Jen.

Her father's original last name was "Boorda," and he was of Russian-Jewish ancestry. She gained Israeli citizenship in 2006, but she represents her birth country, the United States, in international competitions.

Throughout her illustrious career, Bird has won a historic five Olympic gold medals (2004, 2008, 2012, 2016, and 2020). She has also won two NCAA Championships with UConn (2000, 2002); and four FIBA World Cups (2002, 2010, 2014, 2018) and is one of only 11 women to attain all four accolades.
