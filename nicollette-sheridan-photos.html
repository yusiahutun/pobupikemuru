Nicollette Sheridan PICS Hollywood Life

Publish date: 2024-04-07

Nicollette Sheridan, born November 21, 1963, is an actress best known for her role on the ABC series ‘Desperate Housewives.’ Nicolette began her career in the mid-80s, appearing in a number of small roles in film and TV. Her big break came when she landed the role of Paige Matheson / Anne Winston on the series ‘Knots Landing,’ in which she appeared in over 180 episodes between 1986-1993.  

In 2001 she appeared in the series ‘The Legend of Tarzan’ as Eleanor, appearing in over 25 episodes. But her most well known role among American audiences came in 2004 when she earned the role of Edie Britt on ‘Desperate Housewives.’ She earned one Golden Globe nomination for her work. She currently stars on the reboot of ‘Dynasty.’
