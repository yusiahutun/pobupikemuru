Gigi Hadid just wore a wedding dress and its unlike anything we've ever seen

Publish date: 2024-01-29

Wedding inspiration for the supermodel?

    Gigi Hadid would look incredible in pretty much any wedding dress. Be it a flowing boho number, or a fancy fishtail, she would pull it off. The SS19 Moschino show shut down Milan Fashion Week on Thursday evening with Gigi closing the runway in Jeremy Scott's take on a wedding dress and it's the ideal design for rock and roll brides. The mini dress had a double layer bubble hem which was tucked underneath the skirt, giving it a tulip-like appearance. The top part was a Basque-style and unique veil framed her head – tucked into the frock but still leaving a cascading train behind her. Wowzers! Instead of going bare-legged, statement tights were added, and she carried a bouquet of gypsophila blooms.

    Gigi wore a wedding dress at the SS19 Moschino show 

    After the show, Gigi took to Instagram to revel in the big moment in her career and wrote: "@MOSCHINO BRIDE. WOW @ITSJEREMYSCOTT WHAT A MOMENT. To my amazing friend J, I feel so grateful and honoured any time I get to be a part of your creative vision. Every season one of my greatest highlights is getting to learn about your newest story, inspiration, & process. I love your fearlessness, YOUR MIND. When I think you could never be more fun & creative, you do it again. Thank you so much for letting me close this amazing show tonight!!!!!"

    What a veil!

    The question is -will Gigi and boyfriend Zayn Malik tie the knot anytime soon? The couple have been an item for a little over two years and always look extremely loved-up.
