10 things you need to know about Ewan McGregors model daughter Clara

Publish date: 2024-02-03

The 22-year-old is ready to step out of her dad’s shadow

    There’s a new celebrity offspring on the block, and we predict you're going to be hearing rather a lot about her. Ewan McGregor appeared arm-in-arm with his daughter Clara on the red carpet of the Tribeca Film Festival in New York on Monday. And while the star's new film, , was premiering, it was his model daughter who got everyone talking. The 22-year-old looked stunning in a sheer peaches and cream Givenchy frock teamed with 90s-style pointed beige stiletto boots. Here are ten things you need to know about the rising star.

    Clara and her dad appear to enjoy a strong bond

    1. She's not afraid to bare all

    Clara made her modelling debut for magazine just a few days ago. In the feature, the 22-year-old posed nude on a bed and gave a candid interview about her life and ambitions.

    2. She's an aspiring actress

    It appears Clara is keen to follow in her film star dad's footsteps. The New York University graduate told that she wants to pursue an acting career. She's currently starring in a short film that she wrote and directed herself.

    3. She's close with both parents – despite their split

    Clara and her dad looked relaxed and happy on the red carpet together at the Tribeca Film Festival, despite the 47-year-old recently filing for divorce from her mum, designer Eve Mavrakis, and starting a relationship with 33-year-old Mary Elizabeth Winstead. She also appears close to mum Eve, regularly posting pictures of her on her Instagram.

    4. She's one of four sisters

    Clara is an older sibling to Jamyan, 16, Esther, 15, and four-year-old Anouk.

    5. She's been the victim of trolling

    After appearing at Tribeca with her dad, a cruel troll wrote on Instagram: "Dear Clara...where have you left your dignity? Your mother will be taking care of your sisters, will not she? You have sold yourself for a plate of lentils."

    She laughed it off, saying: "Ha! Sorry the last part about the lentils really got me… I love lentils." 

    The model is a fan of the 90s look

    6. She admits to being 'a rebel'

    Clara revealed to Playboy that for years she tried to rebel against her dad by being uninterested in acting: "All throughout high school I really turned my back on acting. I said I was going to be a photographer, I'm going to be behind the lens. At 12-year-old, I moved to LA, which is all Hollywood and all I was asked is, 'Are you going to do what your dad does?' I was rebelling." 

    7. She's proud of her roots

    Clara has been photographed rocking a 'Scotland' logo sweater, proudly referencing her dad's heritage.

    8. She knows how to work the 90s look

    The 22-year-old may not be able to remember much of the 90s, but she certainly knows how to rock a -style miniskirt, crop top and backpack, if pictures from events are anything to go by.

    9. She's an art fan

    Clara regularly posts images of both classical and modern art on her Instagram page, and appears to enjoy photography.

    10. She has famous pals

    Pretty much a requirement for any celebrity offspring: Clara has shared snaps of herself with Victoria's Secret model Martha Hunt and fashion legend Helena Christensen.
