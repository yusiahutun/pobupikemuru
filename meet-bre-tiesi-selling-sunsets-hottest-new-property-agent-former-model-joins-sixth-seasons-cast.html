Meet Bre Tiesi, Selling Sunsets hottest new property agent: the former model joins the sixth seas

Publish date: 2024-04-22

Quinn’s departure from the show last season caused concern among fans regarding the dynamic of the show, but new girl Tiesi seems more than ready to fill her shoes.

In a new trailer for season six, Tiesi already seems to have made an enemy out of fellow agent Chelsea Lazkani. Here’s what we know about the Oppenheim Group’s newest drama queen ...

Bre Tiesi used to be a model

Born and raised in Los Angeles, Tiesi got into modelling while she was still in high school. During an interview with E! News in 2022, she said that her career as a model started quite out of the blue when she was 16 and dating someone who worked in the industry.

She told E! News that her ex’s mum at the time nudged her towards modelling although she had plans to pursue a career as a surgical technician. After landing her first job as a spokesmodel for Rockstar, an energy drink company, Tiesi hasn’t looked back.

She went on to expand her career

Over the years, the 32-year-old has ventured outside modelling and into showbiz. She made appearances on rap battle improv show Wild ’n Out and also started her own brand as a fitness influencer.

With an MMA fighter dad and a nutritionist mum, Tiesi grew up passionate about fitness and has her own workout show Elevate with Bre on OnlyFans TV. She also has a website Body by Bre where she offers fitness programmes.

She was married to American footballer Johnny Manziel

Tiesi tied the knot with her now ex Johnny Manziel in a private ceremony in 2018 after a two-year relationship. Unfortunately, the couple parted ways a year later amid rumours that Manziel had cheated.

She shares a son with TV personality Nick Cannon

Tiesi and Cannon made headlines when it was announced that they were expecting a baby boy in January 2022. Tiesi welcomed her first child and Cannon’s eighth, Legendary Love Cannon, in July of the same year.

The two seemingly had an on-and-off relationship for several years after meeting on the set of Wild ’n Out, which is hosted by Cannon. And while they are not currently dating each other, both seem to share mutual respect and Cannon makes plenty of appearances on the model’s Instagram page.

Fans are anticipating whether their relationship will mean an appearance from Cannon on Selling Sunset.

How she became a property agent

In an interview with People in August 2022, the former model said she decided she wanted to transition out of the modelling industry because she was looking for a long-term career.

“As amazing as modelling was as an industry for me, I was just kind of over it. You know, I’m 31 now. So I’m like, it’s time to be a big girl!” she told People.

Tiesi has already started building a résumé as a property agent. Before joining the Oppenheim Group, she worked at Keller Williams Beverly Hills.

Her beef with Chelsea Lazkani

Drama is already brewing between the O-Group girls and the newcomers, and we’ve only seen the trailers.

In the season six official trailer, it seems that Chelsea Lazkani makes a direct criticism of Tiesi’s relationship with Cannon by saying, “As a Christian, I find Bre’s relationship rather off-putting.”
