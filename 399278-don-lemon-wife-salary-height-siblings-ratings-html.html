Don Lemon's biography

Don was born on 1st March 1966 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana as Donald Carlton Lemon. He is the son of Mr Richardson (father) and Katherine Clark (mother). He has two sisters, namely Yma Lemon and Leisa Lemon.

After his elementary education, Don joined Baker High School in Baker East Baton Rouge Parish. During his senior high school year, he was voted as the class president. Later, he enrolled at Louisiana State University. He graduated with a major in broadcast journalism from Brooklyn College.

Career

Donald began his career while still in college. He used to work for several Fox New affiliates. Upon leaving college, he continued with his journalism career by working as a correspondent for NBS in Chicago and Philadelphia.

He also worked as an investigative reporter and anchor for KTVI in St. Louis, Missouri. In 2003, Don joined NBC's owned and operated station WMAQ-TV as a reporter and local news anchor. While working here, he won three Emmys.
