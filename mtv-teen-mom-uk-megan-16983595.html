Teen Mom UK star distraught as vile troll fakes sister's death

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Just when you thought internet bullies couldn't stoop any lower, they did.

Megan Salmon-Ferrari fell victim just two months ago when she discovered a fake Instagram profile in her name.

Speaking exclusively to Daily Star Online, the mum-of-two revealed the absolutely savage lengths the disgusting troll went to – including faking her own sister Billie's death.

She said: "Someone made a fake account of me on Instagram and put a picture up of my sister and said that she died.

"And my family on my dad's side saw it and thought she was actually dead."

Megan continued: "Obviously I was like 'What are you talking about, she's not dead.'

"They said that she had committed suicide and they put a whole paragraph up."

She continued: "I put a picture of it up on my Instagram and asked everyone to report it."

Sadly that wasn't the only time the teen mum was hit by a wave of nasty social media haters.

Megan added: "When I was pregnant with Dulcie-Mae someone commented saying they swore the baby wasn't Dylan's.

"People were swearing on their lives and I'd never spoken to them or met them.

"A girl also commented on one of my pictures of Mckenzie something really s****y when me and Dylan weren't together."

The MTV reality starlet announced just last week that she's taking a break from using social media due to the sheer number of hate messages.
