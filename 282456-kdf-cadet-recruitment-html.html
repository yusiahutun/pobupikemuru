When is KDF recruitment 2023/2024: details on cadet application

Publish date: 2024-06-08

The armed forces are instrumental to governments because they protect countries from internal and external threats. In Kenya, this duty is assigned to the Kenya Defence Forces (KDF), which comprises the Kenya Navy, Kenya Army, and Kenya Air Force. Every year, KDF recruits staff in various capacities. When is KDF recruitment?

KDF officers include cadets who undergo rigorous training to qualify for their mandate. Each year, hundreds of Kenyans apply for cadet positions. The Ministry of Defence is responsible for conducting KDF cadet recruitment.

When is KDF recruitment? All 2024 details

Every year, thousands of youth show up in selected centres hoping to be selected for the positions of KDF cadets. The cadet recruitment period normally comes with a lot of excitement among young people who desire to join the armed forces.

KDF recruitment

Before the Kenya Defence Forces starts its annual recruitment, the Ministry of Defence makes an official announcement calling for applications.

Cadet application requirements

There are two categories of cadet application, each with different requirements, as explored below.

General Service Officer (GSO) cadets

The applicants must meet the requirements below.

  • Mean grade of B (plain) in KCSE, with C+ (plus) in English, Mathematics, and one science subject.
  • The training period for this category will be three years leading to a Bachelor of Science degree in Defence and Security upon completion.

General requirements for both categories

Besides meeting the requirements above, there are general conditions that prospective cadets must meet. They are listed below.

  • Applicants must be Kenyan citizens.
  • They must be holders of valid Kenyan National Identity (ID) cards. Four copies will be required during the recruitment exercise.
  • Must have a KRA PIN certificate. A copy should be brought during the recruitment exercise.
  • Must be between 18 and 26 years for GSO cadets and general duty recruits.
  • Must have no criminal record.
  • Men must have a minimum height of 1.60m (5ft 3in) and a minimum weight of 54.55 kg (120 lb).
  • Women must have a minimum height of 1.52m (5ft) and a minimum weight of 50.00 kg (110 lb).
  • Both men and women must have a Body Mass Index (BMI) of below 30.
  • Female candidates must not be pregnant at the time of recruitment and during the entire duration of training.
  • All candidates must be medically fit, and their skin must be healthy and devoid of large or deep scars and tattoos.

The recruitment centres are spread across all counties. Each day, the KDF recruitment teams will visit multiple counties at the specified centres.

Remember, all applications will be submitted online through the KDF recruitment portal. Hardcopy applications will be rejected.

You should also note that the recruitment exercise will not be for cadets only. The officials will also recruit specialist officers, general duty personnel, tradesmen/women, and constables within the same period.

How can I join KDF as a cadet?

If you wish to become a KDF cadet and meet all the requirements listed above, you should submit your online application before the stated deadline. Ensure you follow the online application process as indicated in the advert.

Next, wait for the shortlisting process to be completed. If you are shortlisted, you will receive communication from the relevant officials.

Once you receive feedback, confirm when the recruitment team will be visiting your centre and free that day.

Warning on corruption

Many Kenyans believe that the KDF cadet recruitment process is dependent on giving and receiving bribes. KDF prohibits bribery and corruption.

All qualified applicants get an equal opportunity to join the armed forces. Any person caught bribing to be selected as a cadet will lose their money and be sent to jail.

Similarly, persons caught presenting forged academic or other documents will be arrested and prosecuted.

Will there be KDF recruitment?

Yes, the Kenya Defence Forces will be recruiting cadets, specialist officers, general duty personnel, tradesmen/women, and constables between 28th August 2023 and 8th September 2023.

Which month will KDF recruit cadets?

The Kenya Defence Forces will recruit cadets in late August and early September. This will be between 28th August 2023 and 8th September 2023.

On which date does the recruitment of KDF start?

The 2023 KDF recruitment process is scheduled to start in 28th August 2023 and end on 8th September 2023. The relevant officials will visit various recruitment centres across all counties as indicated in the official schedule available on the recruitment portal.
