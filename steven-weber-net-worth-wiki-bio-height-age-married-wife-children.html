Steven Robert Weber’s Bio & Wiki

Steven Weber was born as Steven Robert Weber on 4th March 1961, in Briarwood, Queens, New York, United States. He holds an American nationality and belongs to white ethnicity. Moreover, he is Jewish but, he didn’t receive a formal religious education.

Weber was raised by his mother, Fran, a nightclub singer, and his father, Stuart Weber, a nightclub performer and manager of Borscht Belt comedians. He graduated from Manhattan’s High School of Performing Arts in 1979. Moreover, he enrolled at the State University of New York at Purchase.
