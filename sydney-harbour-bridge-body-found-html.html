Sydney Harbour Bridge body find: Human body is pulled from the water

Publish date: 2024-02-29

A body has been pulled from the water in Sydney Harbour in what is suspected to have been a self-harm incident.

Police were seen near the Ives Steps on the south side of the Harbour Bridge after 3pm on Friday where the man's body was retrieved.

Photos from the scene showed the man's body under a blue tarp aboard a police boat heading back to the shore.  

Several other emergency service vehicles were pictured at the scene.

A NSW Police spokeswoman said the person's death was believed to be a result of self-harm.
