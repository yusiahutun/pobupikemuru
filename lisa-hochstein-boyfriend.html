Lisa Hochstein Boyfriend After Lenny Divorce: Who's Jody Glidden?

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Jody Glidden

Lisa Hochstein and her boyfriend, Jody Glidden, confirmed their relationship in an interview with Entertainment Tonight in February 2023. “I guess it’s sort of out of the bag,” Lisa said at the time. “Yeah, I’m dating. I’m dating this guy. He’s a great guy. He’s been super supportive of me.”

Lisa also explained how Jody is “different to what [she was] used to” with her ex-husband, Lenny Hochstein, whom she split from in 2022 after 13 years of marriage. “[He] has empathy, he cares,” Lisa said of Jody, explaining that her boyfriend “wants to motivate me to do better, be my best. So, it’s a lot of things that I’m not used to dealing with, or used to having from my previous relationship.”

Jody is from Canada, but current lives and works in Miami, Florida, where Lisa lives with her and Lenny’s two children: son Logan and daughter Elle Marie. Jody graduated with his Bachelor in Business Administration degree from the University of News Brunswick. He graduated from Harvard University in 2011 with a master’s degree in management information systems. Jody is also the founder and CEO of Introhive, a tech company he launched in 2012. The company has since been recognized by Deloitte’s Fast 50 and Fast 500 Awards programs for four consecutive years and was named the MarTech Breakthrough Award winner for Best CRM Innovatio in 2020.

Introhive is also the fifth company Jody has founded. He’s also the founder and CEO of Postilize, another tech company that uses AI to change how companies build their base of customers. Like Lisa, Jody is also a parent. He’s the father of one child, a daughter named Peyton, who was born in 2009.

Lisa and Jody went Instagram official in July 2023, five months after Lisa confirmed their romance. “My love ❤️ @miami_jody thanks for being there for me,“ Lisa captioned a photo of her and Jody at the time, to which Jody commented, “I’ll always have your back. Love you baby.”

Jody, for his prt, shared the same photo to his own Instagram, writing, “Sometimes our past is just meant to teach us to be better for our next future. We’ve both experienced a lot and grown a lot and I look forward to so much more. Love you so much.”
