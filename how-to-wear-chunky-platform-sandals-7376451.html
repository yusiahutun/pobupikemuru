10 Chunky Platform Sandal Outfits to Try ASAP

Publish date: 2024-03-27

Practically speaking, the platform shoe trend just makes sense during winter. We'll take all the inches we can get to keep us above rain, slush, and the general muck that's ever-present on the sidewalks during colder seasons. Come spring and summer, our love of platform shoes stays strong, but how we style them shifts dramatically as we say so long to bulky boots and hello to chunky platform sandals outfits (all the short girlies stand up tall!).

There are countless things to love about this shoe trend — seriously, we could write sonnets to platform sandals outfits styled with flatforms, chunky high heels, and wedges — but they can also be a bit of a fashion conundrum. Styling a platform sandals outfit requires balance — literally and stylistically. I mean, the "wrong shoe theory" has been all that fashion editors can talk about, pairing shoes that typically feel wrong for the outfit but surprisingly pull the whole look together. Of course, platform sandals can pair beautifully with a sundress, but there are many other style staples worthy of this inches-adding standout shoe without rushing head-first into warmer weather (global warming has us covered on that front anyway).

Ahead, you'll find 10 ways to style a platform sandals outfit that feels fresh, seasonally appropriate, and totally on-trend.

With a Crochet Dress

Ease in and out of temperature changes by wearing long sleeves under a crochet dress — the trend is having quite the moment — and socks under your fave pair of chunky sandals. Formerly considered a fashion faux pas or a look relegated to the athleisure crowd, socks and sandals are on the trend train, thanks to celebs like Bella Hadid, showing us just how fashion-forward socks and sandals can be.

With Slacks and Socks

Another socks-and-sandals combination serving casual chic is this one, also starring tailored pants. We're all about playing up a menswear vibe by incorporating a collared shirt or a sweater vest. The key here is to create a seamless look, almost as if you were wearing a chunky boot, by keeping the color of your pants, socks, and sandals as closely matched as possible.

With a Sweater Dress and Knee Socks

Sweater dresses are normally thought of as a fall staple, but they can be seamlessly transitioned into your spring wardrobe when worn with a pair of knee socks and your chunkiest platform sandals. Try opting for lightweight socks and a sweater dress in a vibrant shade.

With a Flowy Maxiskirt

Add structure to a boho flowy maxiskirt by slipping into a pair of platform chunky sandals. This look begs for a played-up contrast: The more volume in the skirt, the more utilitarian the vibe of your platform sandals outfit can be.

With Flare Jeans

One way to ensure that your platform sandals outfit is serving cool autumn or early and not jumping the gun to summer style is to simply wear them with long pants. Flares, either in trouser or denim form, are making a comeback, and we wholeheartedly recommend leaning all the way into this flirty and fun silhouette with a pair of equally throwback-inspired platforms.

With a Minidress and Long-Sleeve Shirt

Turn your fave strapless minidress into a platform sandals outfit staple by layering a lightweight long-sleeve knit underneath and adding a matching pair of chunky platform sandals. The vibe is flirty without being overtly revealing, and, depending on the shirt you choose, is a look you can wear year-round as well.

With a Tea-Length Statement Dress

A pair of chunky platform sandals in a texture like suede or velvet is the ideal shoe for a platform sandals outfit that might otherwise read too evening for a daytime look. Go for sandals that complement your statement dress — without matching — to keep it fun and unexpected.

With a Bold Matching Set

Vibrant shades that match your preferred highlighter color from high school are very much a staple this year. They also happen to be a great way to amp up jewel-toned platform sandals outfits. Suit separates work well here, but if you're daring, might we recommend a matching satin pajama set?

With a Floral Midi Dress

Floral dresses run the gamut from flirty to formal, with the overall impression largely dictated by hemlines. A midi- or tea-length floral dress paired with a trench coat and a pair of chunky platform sandals is a look that can be leveled up or toned down to suit your spring occasion.

With Lace Pants

Lace pants are spring's answer to the question: How can I go sheer without technically showing a lot of skin? 

A pair of athletic platform sandals bring a casual air to this sultry trend and create cohesion with the light jacket of your choosing, whether it's a classic bomber or good old-fashioned denim.
