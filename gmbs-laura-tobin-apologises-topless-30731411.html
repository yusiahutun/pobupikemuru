GMB's Laura Tobin apologises for 'topless' sunflower snap and vows to never strip off

Publish date: 2024-02-17

Good Morning Britain star Laura Tobin was forced to apologise after she was reminded of a photo which looked topless in a sunflower field.

During Friday's show the 41-year-old was left red-faced after hosts Adil Ray and Charlotte Hawkins reminded her about her raunchy photo.

While the trio discussed a farmer who had complained about people taking "naked" photos in his sunflower field, Laura previously stated that she would never participate in such an act.

However, Adil and Charlotte were quick to prove her wrong as they showed a photo of Charlotte topless in field of sunflowers.

Kick-starting the conversation, Charlotte said: "I think someone on this programme needs to issue an apology!

She continued: "Just a few moments ago Laura Tobin denied having any naked photos in the sunflowers!

Charlotte then went on to explain the plea from the sunflower farm owner who wants to stop "naked" pics in his field.

The presenter continued: "Laura was at a sunflower farm yesterday, we've just checked with her and she said 'no, no, no, I wouldn't do that'".

Sharing Laura's snap to viewers at home, Charlotte said: "The team have found this, Laura Tobin! What have you got to say for yourself?"

Laura jokingly replied: "Oh, that sunflower photo."

As the studio erupted into laughter, she added: "Sorry farmer, they weltered after that!"

Earlier this week, weather reporter Laura was inundated with support from fans after she announced that she will be working on the show from Monday to Friday- something she hasn't done in a while.

Heading over to Twitter, Laura shared a selfie from the GMB studio and wrote: "Monday-let’s do this. (This is my first full week for a month.)"

Seeing her wholesome post, fans headed straight to the comment section.

One supporter wrote: "You're definitely sunshine on a rainy day as the song goes Laura."

Another fan made a controversial comment and said: "Welcome back Laura. You're the best thing on the show. Such a shame to see GMB slowly morph into GB News."

While a third said: "Good luck Laura, You always do your best at what you do."
