Mandy Moore, Minka Kelly &amp; Nicole Richie Sport Same Dress to Doen Event -- Who Wore It Better?!

Publish date: 2024-08-20

It's a fashion faceoff between three stylish stars!

Famous friends Mandy Moore and Minka Kelly showed up to a celebration for the launch of Dôen in Los Angeles on Thursday night, wearing the same $380 handkerchief dress, one in black and the other in white.

The singer went with the dark version of the midi, which she paired with black sandals, tousled waves and a pretty coral lip. Meanwhile, the "Friday Night Lights" alum wowed in white at the event, adding some bohemian details with tan booties, delicate jewelry and minimal makeup.

But, these BFFs aren't the only celebs who rocked this dress at the soiree!

Nicole Richie put her own spin on the white version of the design by wearing black cage stilettos, a gold bracelet and a braided updo.

All three ladies looked fantastic in the trendy treads, but we have to give the nod to Nicole. We love how she styled the look from head to toe.
