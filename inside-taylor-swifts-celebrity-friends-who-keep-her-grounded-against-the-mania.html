Inside Taylor Swift's celebrity friendships that keep her grounded against the 'mania'

Publish date: 2024-04-01

Taylor Swift’s celebrity friends have guarded her against the maddening attention from the media and also became her best supporters during her lowest moments.

It is often said that maintaining celebrity friendships is tricky, given the constant coverage of them and the twisted narrative that is portrayed. But when it comes to pop music queen Taylor Swift, the star has cracked the secret code to long-lasting friendships with some of the biggest music legends and Hollywood icons.

From Selena Gomez to Emma Stone, Tay Tay’s friendships have blossomed from her teenage years to her mid-thirties and are still going strong.

Taylor Swift’s friends did not invite her to events to avoid ‘attention’

It is not easy being Taylor Swift, who is surrounded by paparazzi everywhere she goes. From stepping out with friends to going out to a private dinner with her beau, the Grammy-winning star took a decade and a half to find that right balance of attention that she could allow in her private life.

But her global fame comes with a price that only she has to pay. The singer once shared how sometimes her friends did not invite her to events knowing there could be paparazzi and helped her out by not including her.

Taylor explained: “Sometimes I don’t get invited to things because my friends know it’s going to be a hassle to take me.”

She added: “I’ll find out that a group of my friends went out to dinner, but they wanted it to be a low-key night and not worry about people coming up to the table every other second and asking for autographs, so they didn’t invite me. I can understand that.”

While it can seem almost ‘rude’ for her friends not to invite her, Taylor understood their intention and took it as a protective measure to actually help her.

Taylor Swift’s closest friend who called her ‘only friend’

Swifties claim that Taylor Swift is the ultimate bff to have to get over your heartbreaks. The singer’s cherished friendships often get the limelight; many of her friends believing the Happiness crooner has been the rock during their not-so-happy moments.

Her closest friend Selena once called the hitmaker her ‘only friend’ in a dire time. “She was frustrated when I was frustrated; she was sad when I was sad,” she revealed.

”I think [my friends] visibly saw me in so much pain. And they didn’t want it for me but they—sorry—they never stopped loving me.”

The feeling is definitely mutual, as Tay never shied away from expressing her love for the Single Soon crooner. Beaming with pride, Tay once said: “The things that she’s overcome, the situations she’s risen above, it’s been a long time since we were friends and she’s the best. She’s just the best.”
