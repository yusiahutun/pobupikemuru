Taniya Nayak’s Early Life

The warm smiled Taniya was born on February 22, 1973, in Nagpur, India. Nayak moved to Boston, Massachusetts at a very young age. Taniya’s father B. D. Nayak is a famous architect in India and Boston whereas her mom is Leela Nayak. Her father being an architect is mostly the main influence behind her passion for designing.

Furthermore, Nayak attended Weymouth High for her high school education and graduated in 1991. After that, she graduated from the University of Massachusetts in Lowell with a bachelors degree in Arts and Marketing. Later, Nayak attended the Boston Architectural College and received a Master’s degree in interior design. During her college years, Nayak stood out as she received a Tops Honors Award in the “Student Sketch Problem Competition” held by Internation Interior Design Association.

Inside of Taniya Nayak’s Career

After Taniya’s graduation, she landed a job on the ABC Family’s show Knock First as a co-host and one of the 4 designers. As successful as her first job was, Nayak wanted to do more. So, Taniya joined the show Designed to Sell aired on HGTV Network and did more practical work of interior designing and decorating houses. This job helped her gain fame and experience to produce her own show.

In 2005, Taniya went to establish her own design company Taniya Nayak Design LLC. She worked on designs for many TV celebrities and music artists as well. Similarly, Nayak’s designs featured in several famous Boston restaurants. While she focused on her company, Nayak guest-starred in shows like Destination Design and House Hunters on Vacation.

Appearing on such shows boosted her fame again and Nayak became a full-blown celebrity. Moreover, Nayak hosted other HGTV shows like Billion Dollar Block and was a regular guest on the Food Network’s show Restaurant: Impossible. This show helped to revive the fortunes of struggling restaurants, and Nayak was all about it.

Furthermore, Taniya was featured in a number of magazines and talk shows. In addition to that, Taniya also received many awards for her elegant and functional designs. Taniya appeared on the Ellen Degeneres Show and became a brand ambassador for Ellen’s QVC line called ED on Air. Likewise, Nayak also hosted the Great Christmas Light Fight on ABC Network once.

Additionally, Nayak won ASID New England’s Excellence in Design Award. Moreover, Taniya featured in various publications such as Architectural Digest India, The Washington Post, and The Boston Globe. Similarly, Nayak appeared on shows like The View and The Oprah Winfrey Show as well.

Taniya Nayak’s Relationships

The famous American-Indian interior designer is married to a Boston restauranteur Brian O’Donnell. Brian is the co-owner of two restaurants in Boston; Zita and Vinalia. Along with that, he is also the manager of a company called Felt.

Before Taniya worked as a designer, she was a bartender for more than 13 years in Boston. The couple met while Nayak bartended at hotspots like Venu, Felt, and Prada. After years of dating, the couple finally tied the knot in 2007.
