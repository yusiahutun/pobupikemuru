What your LIPSTICK shape says about your personality

Publish date: 2024-08-21

When women apply their lipstick, most likely think it's a fairly innocuous task, something that can be done quickly and quietly with the minimum amount of fuss. As it turns out, however, the way in which you apply your lipstick - and the shape that this causes the product to take over time - actually reveals a lot of intimate details about who you are as a person.  

The editors at Good Housekeeping took a look at the variety of different lipstick shapes created by women over time, as the product becomes moulded to their mouth, and revealed the different types of personalities that correspond to each of the most classic shapes. 

From smooth and rounded to blunt and flat, the shape of the lipstick bullet and the way women swipe on their favorite hues says a whole lot about their strengths, weaknesses, and preferences.

Skin deep: Your lipstick shape says a great deal about your personality, according to the beauty experts at a women's magazine

Tip top shape: If this pointed shape is your go-to lipstick, then you're opinionated, and people either love you or hate you

If the tip is rounded to a point

If this is your go-to shape, then you have something of a controversial personality, and people tend to either love you - or hate you. 

You're opinionated, and your not afraid to be vocal about it because you don't care if others are put off by your strong views. Indeed, the sharp aspects of your personality are reflected in the pointed tip of the lipstick.

But despite your bold personality, you are a good and supportive friend, and will always be there when someone you care about needs you.

If the bullet is totally flat on top

Flat top lipstick users are, more often than not, witty, intelligent and charming, according to the editors at Good Housekeeping. 

You tend to have a pretty great sense of humor, and keep your friends entertained with your wit, but you can be stubborn in your views, which can leave your loved ones feeling somewhat frustrated at times.

You're also pretty practical, and would much rather tune into a documentary than sit through a romantic comedy movie.

Flat out: If your lipstick is flat on top (L), you are witty, intelligent and charming, but if your lipstick is rounded (R), you are sensitive and caring

If the lipstick is rounded and smooth 

When you dig into your beauty bag, if you pull out a lipstick that is smooth and rounded in shape, chance are you probably care deeply about your family and much prefer gathering together your friends and loved ones at home, rather than going out.

You're also also very creative and artsy, taking pride in your talents as a writer, musician or artist, and enjoy expressing yourself artistically.

That doesn't mean you're not a stickler for the rules however - quite the opposite in fact. You tend to stay within the bounds of society, and likely have never received so much as a speeding ticket.

If the bullet has a slanted curve 

You're a rule-follower and always have been, hence the more traditional shape of your lipstick. 

You tend to care a lot about what others think of you, and you worry about disappointing people, which is why you're incredibly conscious about every decision that you make day-to-day.

And while those traits can be stressful, they also make you very empathetic and lovable and will  help you to succeed socially, as well as professionally.

Ahead of the curve: Users of a sharp curved shape (L) are empathetic and lovable, while someone who uses a more angled lipstick is the envy of all their friends (R)

If the top is flat, but concave 

If your favorite lipstick is flat at the top but also dips in a little, you are probably extremely motivated and determined, and always finish whatever project you start.

You're probably also intellectually curious, tend to face challenges head on, because you're not one to shy away from risk.

While you were a kid, your rambunctious spirit may have landed you in trouble every now and then, but in your adult years, it has been essential in helping you to gain the confidence needed to pursue important jobs and seek out exactly the kind of spouse you want.

If the sides are sharply-angled 

You go girl.

Chances are if this is your lipstick shape of choice, you're beautiful, intelligent, and a natural flirt - which, unsurprisingly, makes all of your friends a tad jealous.

You're very outgoing and tend to love karaoke, because it gives you a chance to get yourself out there and perform. You also tend to prefer to take adventurous vacations rather than tropical beach trips.

Some people think you're cocky, but deep down you're proud of who you are and you're not afraid to let them know it.

So crushable: People who have a sharp-curved top (L) are loving and always seem to have a new crush, while a sharp diagonal top (R) means you are a natural-born leader and go getter

If the bullet has a sharp curve to it 

If you find many lipsticks with this shape in your cosmetics case, it probably means that you're one of the more romantic people in your friendship circle, and you likely always have a new crush or love interest on the go.

You're very creative, but sometimes you put too much pressure on yourself, which can stifle your creative energies.

Dogs are your favorite animals because, just like you, they are fun and loyal.

If the top slants on a sharp diagonal 

Much like the tip of your lipstick, you don't like to mess around; you're charismatic and ambitious and this has helped you to prove yourself as a natural-born leader in most aspects of your life. 

But far from being big-headed, you're actually incredibly easy to get on with; people enjoy working with you and respect your authority.  

You do, however, love to be the center of attention at parties, and your eagerness to be in control can occasionally irritate your friends.

But at the end of the day, you always manage to make things happen - both in your job and in your social life.
