Taylor Swift 'wouldn't sweep the 2024 Grammys' if Spotify listeners voted, data suggests

Publish date: 2024-04-23

The highly anticipated 66th Annual Grammy Awards will take place in Los Angeles tonight, and predictions are being made on who will take the all-important gramophones home. Taylor Swift, as we know has had an incredible year, receiving an impressive six nominations. However, if it was up to Spotify listeners, she wouldn’t be taking them all home.

The award show will see all our favorite music stars under one arena, battling it out for awards such as Record and Song of the Year. Industry experts decide the votes, but here’s who would be taking the win if the votes were decided by Spotify’s 574 million monthly users, with data gathered from Booking Agent Info’s research team…

Taylor Swift ‘would miss out’ on Album of the year at the Grammys, if Spotify listeners were to decide

Global music streaming service Spotify has a huge 574 million monthly users, and although they do the listening, they have no say in who takes the Grammy Awards home. But if they did, here’s how it would pan out according to the research team at Booking Agent Info, who gathered the streaming data.

Record of The Year – Miley Cyrus – Flowers, with 1,736,263,932 song streams.

SZA comes in second place with 1,626,565,693 streams of Kill Bill, with Taylor Swift in third for Anti-Hero with 1,366,764,290 streams.

Album of The Year – SZA – SOS with 6,703,946,765 album streams in total for all songs.

The same three once again dominate this category on Spotify, with Taylor in second for Midnights with 6,422,331,274 steams. Although Miley Cyrus’ Endless Summer Vacation is in third, it’s miles behind the top two, with 2,463,891,310 album streams in total for all songs.

Best New Artist – Noah Kahan with 35,259,890 monthly listeners.

In terms of listeners, Noah would be closely followed behind by Ice Spice, with 24,700,394 monthly listeners.

However, as we know it’s not the Spotify Listeners who determine the winners, so we’ll have to wait and see what happens tonight.

SZA leads the way in terms of nominations with a staggering nine. One of these is Best Album, alongside Swift. The Love Story singer will become the first artist to win the prize for a fourth time, should she win.

Taylor won’t be performing at the Grammys – but here’s who will

Although rumors were flying about that the Cruel Summer singer would be performing at the event, these have since been shut down. However, fear not, she will still be in attendance, and viewers have an amazing lineup to look forward to.

Performing at the Crypto Arena will be SZA, Burna Boy, Billie Eilish, Billy Joel, Dua Lipa, Olivia Rodrigo, and Travis Scott.

Although Taylor will be in attendance, unfortunately, we won’t be getting the first Red Carpet pics of her and Travis Kelce. The NFL star recently revealed he would not be able to attend due to training for the Super Bowl, taking place in Vegas on February 11.
