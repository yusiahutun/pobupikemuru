Tom Sizemore's missing 'girlfriend' found ALIVE after vanishing nearly six weeks ago

By David Gardner for MailOnline and John Stevens for the Daily Mail
Updated: 12:19 EST, 15 May 2011

1

View
comments


The missing actress linked to Hollywood tough guy actor Tom Sizemore has been found alive, it has emerged.

Megan Wren was found in a house in the Rampart area of Los Angeles, close to where she had disappeared nearly six weeks ago.

Saving Private Ryan star Mr Sizemore said he was 'relieved and happy' that she was safe. It is not yet clear if he was involved in her disappearance or not.

Missing person: Police plan to question Tom Sizemore over the disappearance of 25-year-old Megan Wren. She was last seen on March 31

Police went to the address where Miss Wren had been staying after the 25-year-old actress made contact with her family to tell them where she was.

Officers said she had been staying with friends all along.

The actress was found as police prepared to question Mr Sizemore a second time in connection with the disappearance.

While officers insisted the ‘Natural Born Killers’ star was not a suspect, fears had grown for missing Megan Wren since she vanished nearly six weeks ago.

'I am relieved and happy Megan is safe and sound,' Mr Sizemore told MailOnline.

'It is unfortunate that she did not come forward immediately as this has caused me a lot of concern.

'However, I am pleased no harm has come to Megan and I wish her and her family the best and I am putting this matter behind me.'

'Tom has been helping her out,' his manager Charles Lago told MailOnline.

Megan is now with her father, he said.

Aspirations: Miss Wren hoped to be an actress

The 49-year-old actor is adamant that Miss Wren was not his girlfriend.

He has also denied reports that he was the last person to see her before she disappeared on March 31.

The ‘Saving Private Ryan’ star’s manager Charles Lago said he had made three offers to police to search Mr Sizemore’s apartment.

Hollywood star: Sizemore in the 1998 film Saving Private Ryan

‘I made the offers so they could eliminate him from their enquiries, but they said they weren’t interested,’ he told MailOnline.

‘The girl is not Tom’s girlfriend,’ he added. ‘She is a girl he knows.'

‘During the time that she disappeared, Tom was on a movie set. The police have checked it all out. He stayed in a hotel and he wasn’t even home.’

The movie he was shooting was called Slumber Party Slaughter.

Mr Lago insisted yesterday that claims that Miss Wren’s family couldn’t reach the actor were untrue.

‘Tom is in Germany at the moment and he has run up a $1,200 telephone while he’s there. I asked him how it got so high and he said he’d been calling Megan’s father because he was so worried about her,' said Mr Lago.

‘He has known her for a while because they lived in the same building.

‘He said she has never been his girlfriend and she certainly hasn’t been in the one year and three months since I have been representing him. Whenever I visited him and she was there I would ask her to leave and then he would let her back in again.'

He confirmed that the actor had talked to the police and was happy to do anything he could to help find Miss Wren.

Detective Carmine Sasso, the officer in charge of the Los Angeles Police Department missing persons unit, told MailOnline yesterday: ‘I have spoken to Mr Sizemore and he provided us with a preliminary statement.  

'He has been cooperative and responsive. At some point, we would like to talk to him again.'

Detective Sasso wouldn’t comment on claims that the Megan had a drug problem, citing patient confidentiality.

Not well: Ms Wren's family told police she has a 'serious medical condition, and is in need of medical care'

An LAPD statement yesterday said the family were 'very concerned about her unexplained disappearance, especially given the fact that she has a serious medical condition, and is need of medical care'.

Her grandmother, Georgiana Wren, had appealed for help, saying: ‘The family is extremely worried about her, especially since she’s in poor health. Someone knows something. Please help us and help her.’

Miss Wren was last seen around 3pm in the Rampart area of Los Angeles, near Vermont Avenue and Wilshire Boulevard, according to the LAPD.

Detective Sasso said: 'The LAPD handles 3,200 adult missing persons per year, and a very high percentage are cleared, so the odds are in our favour.

‘A very low percentage results in foul play. In the last 24 months, 10 evolved into homicides, all of which were solved'.

Mr Sizemore previously dated Hollywood Madam Heidi Fleiss.

He was convicted of assault and battery against her in 2003.

They appeared together in the third season of Celebrity Rehab with Dr.  Drew last year where he tackled his long battle with drug addiction.

He described himself as sober during an interview with The Fix, a website about addiction and recovery, that was posted earlier this year.

The star said: 'I’ve got three priorities. One: stay clean. Two: stay clean. And three: stay clean.'

He has a history of drug related arrests, including possession of methamphetamine, and once admitted to using a prosthetic penis in a bid to fake a urine test.
