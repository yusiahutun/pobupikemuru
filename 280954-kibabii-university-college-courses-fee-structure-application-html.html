Kibabii University requirements

The general admission requirements are as follows:

  • A minimum KCSE mean grade of C+ with at least C+ in the relevant subjects.
  • Kibabii University admission letter.
  • Two photocopies of the certificate/result slip.
  • Students' acceptance of admission offer form.
  • Signed declaration of student regulations.
  • One copy of the completed student personal details form
  • Entrance medical examination form
  • Original national identity card and a copy or birth certificate.
  • Receipts for required fees from the finance department.

Kibabii University online application

Here is a step-by-step guide on how you can apply for a course at Kibabii University.

  • Go to the official website of Kibabii University.
  • Click on "Apply Now".
  • Click "Create Account" and fill out the form with your details.
  • Once you have created an account, fill out the online application form accurately. Provide all the required information, including personal details, academic history, and program preferences.
  • There may be an application fee that you need to pay online.
  • After filling out the form and uploading all necessary documents, review your application to ensure accuracy. Then, submit it through the online portal.

    Is Kibabii University a public university?

    Kibabii University (KIBU) is a public university located in the western part of Kenya in Bungoma County.

    How to download Kibabii University admission letter?

    Follow the procedure below to download your admission letter and other documents.

  • Visit the official website of Kibabii University.
  • Locate and click on the "Log In" button.
  • Enter the email address you used to create your account and your password, then click on the "Log In" button to access your account.
  • Look for the option to download your admission letter and click on it to initiate the download process.
  • After downloading your admission letter, download all other documents provided.
  • Once you have downloaded all the necessary documents, open each and fill in the required information accurately.
  • After filling out the documents (KIBU 1, KIBU 2, KIBU 3, and KIBU 4), scan them using a scanner or take clear photos of each document.
  • Log in to your account on the university website again.
  • Look for the "My Documents" section or "Uploaded Documents."
  • Click on the option to upload documents and select your filled admission documents' scanned or photographed files.
  • Follow the prompts to complete the upload process, ensuring all documents are successfully uploaded.
  • Once uploaded, verify that all documents are correctly displayed in your account's "Uploaded Documents" section.

    Where is Kibabii University College located?

    Kibabii University College has in its main campus in Bungoma County along Chwele-Kimilili road and is next to Cardinal Otunga Girls Bungoma and Kibabii Boys High School. The university also has a learning center in Turkana located along the Lodwar-Lokichongio road in Kakuma.

    Kibabii University College is among the youngest university in Kenya. But, despite its age, the university has invested in modern facilities including a library, lecture theaters, and hostels.
