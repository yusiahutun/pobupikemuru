Tommy Fury admits he's lost his six pack and says having a ripped body 'doesn't mean anything'

Publish date: 2024-05-08

Tommy Fury has admitted he's lost his six pack and says having a ripped body 'doesn't mean anything' after Molly-Mae Hague poked fun at his plump stomach.

The Love Island runner-up, 20, says he isn't bothered about having chiseled abs now he's in a long-term relationship with Molly – and insists he didn't go on the ITV2 dating series to show off his physique. 

Speaking exclusively to MailOnline, reality star and sportsman Tommy said: 'I wouldn't say I have a six pack anymore. I don't care about having a six pack. It wasn't important to me on Love Island either.'

Speaking of his boxer brother Tyson, Tommy continued: 'Tyson doesn't have a six pack but he's the best heavyweight in the world, so it doesn't mean anything. 

'I was in good shape on the show but at the end of the day Love Island wasn't a body building contest. It was about finding a girlfriend and falling in love.
