    How to Choose the Best Kilian Perfume?

    Below are some points you must consider to get the most from Kilian perfumes. 

    Discovering Your Signature Smell 

    Everyone has a different taste when it comes to perfumes. Some prefer delicate floral fragrances, while some lean towards citrusy and woodsy note aromas. So you have to determine your scent choice to simplify the selection process. 

    Identifying the Perfume's Strength  

    Kilian Paris manufactures both strong and light-scented perfumes. Typically, strong-scented formulas go well with special occasions, while light ones are apt for everyday use. However, if you're smell-sensitive, you should avoid strong perfumes and stick to light and delicate scents. 

    Knowing the Formula's Longevity 

    Some formulas last for a couple of hours, while others may stay for a day or two. Generally, heavy and intense fragrances have a higher longevity than lighter ones. That's due to ingredient concentration. If you prefer bold and strong scents, you can choose a more concentrated formula. However, the case is reversed with light-scented blends.

    How to Use a Kilian Perfume?

    After finding your signature perfume, here's how you can use it correctly. 

  • Apply the formula directly onto your skin, including pulse points such as wrists, neck, or behind the ears. 
  • Don't rub your wrists until the liquid dries. 
  • Avoid overapplying it. Just use the required amount, as excessive concentration may negatively trigger your senses, especially if you're smell-sensitive. 
  • Don't apply highly concentrated lotions, oils, or moisturizers if you want to embrace the original smell.
