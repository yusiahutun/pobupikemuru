Kylie Jenner Channels Her Inner Mermaid in an Iridescent Aqua Gown

Publish date: 2024-03-19

Summer came early for the Kardashian-Jenner family, who are enjoying a tropical island vacation. As we all yearn for warm weather here on the East Coast, Kylie Jenner gave us plenty to dream about. In her latest Instagram post, she hit the summertime trifecta: sandy beaches, golden sunsets, and mermaidcore fashion.

Instagram

courtesy of Kylie Jenner via Instagram

In the photos, the Khy founder poses on a beach in front of a glorious orange sky, wearing a breathtaking glittery aqua gown that featured a high scoop neckline, long sleeves, a floor-length skirt, and a form-fitting construction that revealed an outline of the triangle bikini Jenner wore underneath. She glistened like a princess as her shimmery Galvan dress caught the light; she allowed it to soak up all the glory by eschewing jewelry and accessories. As for beauty, she accentuated her sun-kissed glow with bronzy, dewy makeup, complete with a glossy natural-pink lip, and slicked her hair back into a sleek bun. She simply captioned the carousel of images: “twilight.”

Instagram

The makeup mogul gave her 400 million followers another peek into her trip yesterday when she shared a series of sizzling photos of herself in a sexy black Chanel bikini. The set consisted of stringy, high-cut bottoms and a triangle top, both of which were covered in shiny silver sequins. Jenner showed off her killer figure, posing in the ocean and by some rocks along the shore. She also included close-ups of the beachy day’s details, including her black and beige knit tote bags with her summer essentials and her yellow, blue, and purple holographic manicure and statement rings.

Jenner’s sisters Kim Kardashian and Khloé Kardashian shared their own beachy pics. In an uncaptioned Instagram slideshow, Kim poses in a white halter bra, matching briefs and a light gray skirt, all from Miu Miu, in front of a sunset. Khloé, meanwhile, stunned in a bronze one-piece with stud detailing throughout as she cooled off in beautiful clear waters.
