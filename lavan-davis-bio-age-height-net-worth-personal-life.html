LaVan Davis Professional Life

Davis started his acting career in 2004 by playing the role of Leroy in the film, Mindbenders. Likewise, the same year, LaVan starred as Curtis Payne, in the TBS sitcom Tyler Perry’s House of Payne.

Davis worked with Perry in two of his feature films, Daddy’s Little Girls in 2007 and Tyler Perry’s Meet the Browns in 2008. Besides his work in films and television, Davis also appeared in the stage plays like Why Did I Get Married? as Poppy, and Madea Goes to Jail as Leo.
