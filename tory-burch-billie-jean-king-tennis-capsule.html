Shop Tory Burch's Exclusive Billie Jean King Cup Capsule

Publish date: 2024-03-15

Tennis fashion is more than just one of many potential American sportswear references for designer Tory Burch. The designer's first tennis collection predates the current tenniscore movement by seven years (it arrived in 2015). Burch also grew up playing tennis and watching Billie Jean King's trailblazing ascent to the top of the sport.

With all that history, Burch's latest collaboration hits different than the average tennis line. In June, Burch (the woman) partnered with Billie Jean King herself to announce that Burch (the brand) would be the official outfitter of the Billie Jean King Cup by Gainbridge. Also known as the tennis Women's World Cup, it's the largest international women's team event, running this year from November 8 to 13. And in its honor, the pair have released a capsule of tennis essentials to shop now.

When it came to channeling King's legendary career into a capsule, "I was definitely inspired by what I saw [her] wearing in the 1970s," Burch tells BAZAAR.com. "I wanted to design chic pieces with a bit of a retro sensibility." This translates to a lineup of classics with exclusive flourishes: chevron stripes, racquet embroidery, and nods to the Billie Jean King Cup logo, all in a palette of court-appropriate white, green, and navy.

What else makes this tennis capsule feel special? There are the details—"We focus on innovation around materials, while being meticulous about fit and comfort," Burch says—and there's a do-good element. Proceeds from each piece benefit the Tory Burch Foundation and the Billie Jean King Leadership Initiative.

A central element of Tory Burch's partnership with King isn't shoppable, and all the more significant for it. Burch designed a winner's jacket for the first-place team at the Billie Jean King Cup. The jacket comes in a special shade named Billie Blue, referencing a color King wore throughout her career, including the infamous "Battle of the Sexes" match in 1973.

Tory Burch

The garment is the very first champion jacket for a women-led sporting event. Winners who get to wear it will find a special message from King stitched on the inside. "It's not just a jacket," King says in an interview, which you can watch above. "It's got a lot of love and kindness and design and thought into it."

Noa Griffel

A lifelong love of tennis went into this collection, but beginners, don't worry. If you're a novice—or just interested in the courtside aesthetic—you're more than welcome to wear the collection. As Burch says, "It is inspiring to see women of all ages embracing tennis clothing and wearing different pieces on and off the court."

The Tory Burch x Billie Jean King collection is available now online at toryburch.com.
