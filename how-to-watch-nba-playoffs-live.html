Calling all basketball fans, the NBA postseason is here, and it’s looking like another action-packed playoffs. If you’re getting your jerseys and brackets out, you’re probably also wondering how to watch the NBA playoffs live for free. There are plenty of channels and streaming options, which we’ve laid out for you so you can catch every single tip-off, buzzer-beater, and comeback in the postseason.

Each year, the National Basketball Association (NBA) playoffs determine the league champion. The elimination tournament features a four-rounds, best-of-seven format. Six teams from each of the two conferences automatically earn spots in the NBA playoffs, while the final two playoff seeds are decided in a play-in tournament. After the first round comes the conference semifinals and then the conference finals. The two winning teams from the conference finals will go head to head in the NBA Finals starting on June 6.

    Which teams are in the NBA playoffs?

    So which teams are in the 2024 NBA playoffs? Six teams from each conference (the Eastern and the Western) automatically qualify for the playoffs based on their regular season winning percentage. Teams that finished seven through 10 from each conference face off in the play-in tournament to determine who secures the final two playoff seeds. The winner of the Eastern Conference and the winner of the Western Conference compete against each other in the NBA Finals starting on June 6.
