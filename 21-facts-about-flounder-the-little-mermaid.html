Flounder is Ariel’s loyal sidekick.

Flounder is a character from Disney’s animated classic, “The Little Mermaid.” He is inseparable from Ariel, the mermaid princess, and accompanies her on her underwater adventures.

Flounder is a tropical fish.

He is depicted as a bright yellow and blue tropical fish, with distinctive big eyes and a friendly smile.

Flounder is known for his cautious nature.

Unlike his adventurous friend Ariel, Flounder tends to be more cautious and often tries to dissuade her from taking risks.

Flounder loves to explore the underwater world.

Despite his cautious nature, Flounder has a curiosity for the wonders of the underwater world, which leads him to join Ariel on her adventures.

Disney theme parks, such as Disneyland and Disney World, have included Flounder in various Little Mermaid-themed attractions and shows.

Flounder’s design was inspired by a real species of fish.

The animators took inspiration from the California sheephead fish, which influenced Flounder’s unique coloration and facial features.

Flounder appears in other Disney media.

Besides “The Little Mermaid,” Flounder has made appearances in other Disney television shows and films, further extending his presence in the Disney universe.
