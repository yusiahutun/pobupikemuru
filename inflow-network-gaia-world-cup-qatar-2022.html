INFLOW Network &amp; GAIA: One of the Agencies of World Cup Qatar 2022

Publish date: 2024-04-10

Founded in 2016 as INFLOW Travel Summit & transformed into INFLOW Network in 2021, INFLOW Network has been creating events all over the world to improve and shape Influencer Marketing sector through conference based events where Influencers from several countries & all segments come together with sector professionals, and brand representatives to learn and talk about partnerships. With its worldwide presence and network, INFLOW keeps being the key role player in the digital world.

Defining creativity as “bringing different points together in a genuine fashion”, GAIA creates experiences where brands can achieve their full potential. Rooted long before it was established, GAIA as an agency values the ideas that generate curiosity, and carries the digital works to a new level. At GAIA, it is not about adapting to digital, it’s about creating already digital ideas to succeed.

Working together under one roof, we as INFLOW & GAIA are proud to announce that we will be working as the official digital agency in Arabic speaking countries for World Cup Qatar 2022. You can find out more on GAIA’s Medium.

An amazing and long journey is ahead! So join us on this road for a wild trip! Come chat with us on our social media to celebrate & ask your questions.
