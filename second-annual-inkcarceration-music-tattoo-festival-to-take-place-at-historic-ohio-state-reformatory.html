Second Annual INKCARCERATION Music &amp; Tattoo Festival To Take Place At Historic Ohio State Reformator

Publish date: 2024-09-19


MANSFIELD, OH (CelebrityAccess) – INKCARCERATION Music and Tattoo Festival has today (Nov. 15) announced that it will be returning for a second year in 2019.

Taking place once again in Mansfield, OH at the historic Ohio State Reformatory––one of the most haunted buildings in the country and the famed setting of the 1994 film The Shawshank Redemption ––INKCARCERATION 2019 will take place July 12, 13 and 14 and feature over 30 bands. Additionally, the festival will once again feature over 70 tattoo artists from across the country, a large variety of food trucks and beverages, and a haunted house, which has been dubbed ‘Escape From Blood Prison.’

“The Reformatory is thrilled for the return of the INKCARCERATION Music and Tattoo Festival in 2019,” said, Ohio State Reformatory Creative Marketing Director Dan Smith. “We had the chance to take a behind the scenes look at the upcoming lineup for this year and it is epic. You are not going want to miss this! Summer months are made to be enjoyed outdoors, and we can’t think of a better time than with live music and the opportunity to get a tattoo right here at the Shawshank Prison. Come prepared for a great time that you’ll be talking about all year.”

The official INKCARCERATION 2019 band line-up is scheduled to be announced in January.
