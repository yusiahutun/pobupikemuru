Meet Dennis McKinley Mother, Gina Clayton-Lane

Gina Clayton-Lane is best known as the mother of Dennis McKinley. If you’re also not familiar with Dennis then, he is the baby-father/ex-fiance of Porsha Williams from Family Matters. Together Dennis and Porsha share a daughter named Pilar Jhena McKinley who was born on 22 March 2019.

Coming back to Gina, she is a mother of three kids. She writes on her IG and Facebook bio, she is a mother of three kids, grandmother of four, and an aunt of many. She had her old son Dennis on 24 July 1980. On his 38th birthday in 2018, Gina wrote, “Happy 38th Birthday to my wonderful Entrepreneur…My Eldest Son! You’re a blessing to All! Especially to Me! You’re awesome! You’re the best! May God continue to bless you and fill your life with greatness and prosperity! Have a restful, fun-filled day! #Proudmom #blessed #greatboss”.

Then Gina had her second son Quan Clayton who turned 30 years old in March 2021. She wrote on her IG in March 2019, “Happy 28th Birthday to my awesome, fun, and loving son Jacquan! May God continue to bless you with all your heart desires!. Have a wonderful day!”

Quan is available on Instagram as well as Facebook. His IG (@tnobey) is private with 488 followers. He is also on TikTok (@King_dadjokes_). And, his Facebook account is (@quan.clayton.9) states that he worked at Panera Bread. He is currently working as General Manager/Owner at Queen Virgin Remy Birmingham.

Quan has two daughters Milan and Khloe who shares the same birthday (20 May).

Besides two sons, Gina also has a daughter named Desharra McKinley Sherman. (@msdesharra), her IG is private at the moment. Her IG bio states that she is a Registered Nurse by profession and loves fashion. Moreover, her Facebook reveals that she lives in Southfield, Michigan. She has a kid but is currently single.

Desharra celebrates her birthday on 8 October every year.

Gina Clayton-Lane Husband

According to Gina’s eldest son Dennis, Gina Clayton-Lane was previously in a relationship/married to Dennis McKinley Sr. She had Dennis and Desharra with McKinley Sr.

On Father’s day 2015, Dennis wrote on her Facebook, “Father’s Day is a special day I believe in recognizing excellence My uncle Elton did a great job My stepfather also did a great job My cousins are excellent fathers. My friends are excellent I salute them and all fathers who no matter how trying a job it stay the course and does their duties.”

He concluded, “Dennis Sr sucked & failed so he gets no love today.” After the relationship between Gina and Dennis Sr. ended, she met her second husband Bernard Lane. It is not known when did they get married or did they have Quan together, but it appears he played a great role in being a father figure to Gina’s kids. Gina has posted a video of her spouse helping her granddaughter out with her work, which is likely Bernard. Sources revealed, Bernard Rex Lane is 65 years old who was born on May 30. He currently lives in Southfield, Michigan.

Gina Clayton-Lane Age

Based on her IG post, Gina Clayton-Lane is 61 years old as of Jan 2021. She celebrates her birthday on 21 June.

Gina Clayton-Lane Job

Gina Clayton-Lane is on LinkedIn. Her profile revealed that she has been the CEO of The Original Hot Dog Factory since February 2018. She works as a Franchising, Training, Operations, and Inservice Instructor and her business is thriving extensively because there are many franchises.

Before that, Gina was working as Sales Manager at Queen Virgin Remy Exotic Virgin and Remy Hair Extensions. Her job in the company lasted for 5 years from April 2013-March 2018. Her longest job to date was Health Unit Coordinator working at a Hospital from April 1993 to January 2014.

About her education, Gina graduated from Wayne State University with Bachelor’s degree in management in 1984. In 1988, she joined Kranz Woods Academy of Laboratory Technology as an Associate of Science – AS Clinical/Medical Laboratory Technician.

She attended Henry Ford Community College and earned an Associate degree in Health Care.
