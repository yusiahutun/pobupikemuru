5 facts about Francis Antetokounmpo

Unlike his brothers, who are mainly athletes, Francis is also a musician. This gives him a unique distinction and diversity from the one created by his family. As a result, many of his fans are more curious about his personal life. Here are some detailed facts about him that you need to know.

1. Francis was born on October 20th, 1988

Francis Olowu Ofili Adetokunbo was born on October 20th, 1988, in Lagos, Nigeria, to Veronica and Charles Adetokunbo. Therefore, he is 33 years old as of January 2022, and his birth sign is Libra. His parents moved to Athens, Greece, when he was young. However, he remained in Nigeria for a while with his grandparents.

Francis, however, reunited with his family later. Even though he and his brothers were raised outside Nigeria, they are adept at her culture and customs.

2. Francis has played soccer and basketball.

Like his father, a former football player in Nigeria, Francis pursued soccer as a career. He also played basketball for a while. However, unlike the rest of the Antetokuonmpo brothers, his passion for football was greater.

While in Greece and Nigeria, Francis played as a midfielder. He signed with AE Sparti soccer club in Sparta, Laconia, in January 2018. He played for the club for a few months before quitting. In September of the same year, he joined the Aittitos Spaton but also left the team the following year.

After moving to the United States, Francis Antetokounmpo soccer profession did not go professionally.

3. Francis is also a musician.

What does Francis Antetokounmpo do? Being the most diverse in his family regarding careers, Francis has pursued several paths, including music. Despite being in an athletic household, he has proved to have a spot for music in his heart. His passion for music stems from his mother Veronica, who was a music lover and even thought that she could sing.

Though his family name Antetokuonmpo is famously known, Francis does not use it as his artistic name. Instead, he uses his middle and native name Ofili. On July 22nd, 2020, he released his first song, Shekosi.

The song features a guest star, the Serbian actor Darko Peric. The song was widely received and appreciated by his audience. He has since posted several music videos on his channel, and his music career seems promising.

4. He is the firstborn in the family and has four siblings

Who is the oldest Antetokounmpo? Francis is the firstborn even though Gianni Antetokuonmpo is the most famous of the brothers. Gianni was drafted 15th by the NBA. He currently plays for the Milwaukee Bucks. His style and speed have seen him dubbed 'The Greek Freak'.

Giannis Antetokounmpo and Francis Antetokounmpo played for the Filathlitikos youth team back in Athens. Nonetheless, the Antetokounmpo family has five sons. The four siblings are Thanasis, Giannis, Kostas, and Alex. Together, they participate in various charity events in Greece and the United States.
