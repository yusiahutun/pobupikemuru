Dua Lipa's Searing-Hot Late-Night Style Involves a Statement Micro Miniskirt

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Dua Lipa put the spotlight on her searing-hot style during a recent guest appearance on The Tonight Show.

The Grammy winner looked fabulous while chatting with late-night host Jimmy Fallon in New York City, where she turned up to the set in a monochromatic red outfit from Valentino, punctuated with black elements.

Maison Margiela Black Tabi Knee-High Boots

Maison Margiela Black Tabi Knee-High Boots

This included a sharply tailored button-down awash in a blazing shade of red, featuring an oversize silhouette and concealed button closures. Fittingly, she paired the red top with a matching crimson miniskirt made out of delicate flower-shaped embroidery stitched together. She layered the skirt over sheer black tights, and topped it off with an incredible pair of black leather knee-high Tabi boots by Maison Margiela. Her accessories included shiny gold hoop earrings with a draping chain detail and a few gold rings.

During her Tonight Show appearance, Lipa talked to Fallon about the highly anticipated release of her forthcoming third studio album.

“I don’t want to give too much away. I’m not ready yet to come and tell you my album title,” she teased. “I’ll come back next time, and I’ll spill all the tea.”

Asked what “vibe” she’s aiming for with her next project, she said, “I’m going for more of like a psychedelic pop vibe. I’m still trying to find the language to talk about the album without talking about the album. … This new record, I made it with my friends Kevin Parker, Tobias Jesso Jr., Danny Harle, and Caroline Ailin. And it was kind of made like a band.”

She continued, “I just wanted to have that kind of really organic element that I had while I was on tour, because when I was touring for a whole year, I just started to fall in love with the songs and the live element. And I was like: I just want to bring all of that into this new record.”
