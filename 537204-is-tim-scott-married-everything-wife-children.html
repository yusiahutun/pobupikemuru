Who is Tim Scott?

Timothy Eugene Scott is an American businessman and politician. He is a national leader who has served as the junior United States senator for South Carolina since 2013. Tim is a member of the Republican Party, where he previously served as a Charleston city councillor, a state representative, and a US Representative.

He also worked in financial services before entering politics. Senator Tim is a member of the Seacoast Church of Charleston.

Tim was born on 19 September 1965 in North Charleston, South Carolina, to Frances, a nursing assistant, and Ben Scott Sr. He has an older brother who is a sergeant major in the US Army.

Is Tim Scott married?

Was Tim Scott ever married? No. The senator is not married and has never been married before. He also has no children. However, Tim is engaged and is due to be married. In January 2024, he surprised fans when he announced his engagement to his girlfriend Mindy on social media.
