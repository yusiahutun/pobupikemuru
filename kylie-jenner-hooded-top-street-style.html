Kylie Jenners Dazzling Party Look Involves a Hooded Top

Publish date: 2024-05-20

Kylie Jenner is partying like it's 2002.

The Kylie Cosmetics founder recently embarked on a night out in a dazzling party outfit that channeled the early aughts. In particular, her sparkling silver Paco Rabanne top, which featured a cowl neckline and a hood, was reminiscent of Paris Hilton's iconic chain-mail 21st birthday dress. She paired the statement piece with grunge striped patchwork jeans from Sevali and further accessorized with a silver mini bag from Hermès.

For glam, Jenner opted for a sultry yet subtle smoky eye and glossy lip. She also wore her hair in delicate waves under the top's hood.

Sharing photos of the jaw-dropping ensemble on Instagram, she wrote in the caption, "forever i'm that girl !!!!"

Instagram

Instagram

The reality TV star has been getting more and more experimental with her street style looks. For instance, during a recent date night with baby daddy Travis Scott, Jenner looked edgy in a leather biker jacket, which featured collarbone cutouts, a cropped triangular hemline, padded sleeves, and a circular silver zipper charm.

For her bottoms, she once again opted for unconventional denim, wearing a distressed miniskirt composed of two different shades of denim sewn together and completed with a frayed hem and visible pocket. She capped the look off with black shield sunglasses and strappy tie-up pumps.

During another outing with friends this past July, Jenner filmed a TikTok of her outfit, the centerpiece of which was a Madonna-inspired satin minidress with a conical bust. She accessorized with diamond jewelry.
