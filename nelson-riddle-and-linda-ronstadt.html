About

American Composer Nelson Riddle was born Nelson Smock Riddle Jr. on 1st June, 1921 in Oradell, NJ, USA and passed away on 6th Oct 1985 Los Angeles, CA, USA aged 64. He is most remembered for 1950s work for Capitol Records, providing arrangements and musical direction to such vocalists as Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, Nat King Cole, Judy Garland, Peggy Lee and Keely Smith. His zodiac sign is Gemini.

Linda Ronstadt is a 77 year old American Singer. Born Linda Marie Ronstadt on 15th July, 1946 in Tucson, Arizona, USA, she is famous for having released several albums like Hand Sown... Home Grown, Heart Like a Wheel, Hummin’ to Myself, Prisoner in Disguise, Dedicated to the One I Love, Canciones de Mi Padre, and Cry Like a Rainstorm, Howl Like the Wind in a career that spans 1965–2011. Her zodiac sign is Cancer.
