See All of Noah Cyrus's Edgy Paris Fashion Week Looks

Publish date: 2024-03-04

Noah Cyrus's street style during Paris Fashion Week is giving off major main-character energy.

The singer and little sister of pop star Miley Cyrus has been fashion show-hopping in the City of Light this week. She attended the Weinsanto fall/winter 2023 runway show last Monday, sat front row at Off-White's fall/winter show on Thursday, and was one of the celebrity VIPs at the Didu Paris fall/winter show on Friday. And yesterday, she performed at the Neiman Marcus Awards to wrap up the week.

Pierre Suu//Getty Images

To celebrate Weinsanto, Cyrus wore an edgy, washed-out green bodysuit with an attached skirt. The piece featured a marble-like print and had a turtleneck, long sleeves that doubled as gloves, and leggings that covered the musician’s pointed-toe heels. The look didn’t leave much space for accessories, but Cyrus added a shimmery pop with a pair of silver hoops.

She left her inky-black straight hair loose for the outing and wore pink eye shadow up to her bleached brows, peach blush, and a glossy rosy lip.

Arnold Jerocki//Getty Images

At Off-White's event, Cyrus honored late creative director Virgil Abloh in a black-and-white hooded minidress. The long-sleeved piece was embellished with lace flowers throughout, which were layered over an abstract print made up of polka dots in different sizes and a netted pattern. The sleeves of the dress doubled as fingerless gloves, and the piece additionally featured a sash on one side of the skirt.

The "I Got So High That I Saw Jesus" singer wore the mini with black sheer tights and knee-high black leather heeled boots. As for glam, she had her hair slicked back and wore rosy, pink blush, and a matching lip tint to go with her pointy powder pink manicure.

Vittorio Zunino Celotto//Getty Images

For the Didu Paris show, Cyrus opted for a vintage-inspired look with a ’70s-esque brown denim dress with dramatic shoulder pads and white faux fur on the sleeves. A long zipper ran from the bust all the way down to the skirt, which featured a high leg slit and a raw hem. Large pockets adorned the sides, and Cyrus’s waist was cinched with a built-in belt with a large silver buckle.

Cyrus wore the unique piece with long black leather boots with round toes. On this occasion, she let her hair down in a silky straight ’do and wore barely-there makeup.

BFA

For her Sunday performance at the Neiman Marcus Awards, Cyrus wore a breezy red chiffon dress by Alexandre Vauthier. It featured layers of sheer fabric that cascaded down to the floor and revealed the matching bodysuit Cyrus wore underneath. She paired the dress with a matching red chiffon headscarf and a red metallic ring. Her makeup was rosy and minimal.
