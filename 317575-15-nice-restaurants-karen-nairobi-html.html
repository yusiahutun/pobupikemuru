15 nice restaurants in Karen, Nairobi

Publish date: 2024-02-21

Apart from wildlife, Kenya is also famous for its vibrant art. The country has its fair share of cute, peaceful, and artsy restaurants that are mostly designed to represent the local culture. If you plan on ever finding yourself in the affluent Karen neighbourhood or if you live around the area and want to try out a few excellent restaurants, then a list of restaurants in Karen will come in handy.

Although the Karen neighbourhood has its fair share of eateries, not all are worth the hype. The good thing is that there are varied restaurants, each with a unique theme or cuisine that makes it stand out from the rest. Despite this, only a handful of them deliver a combination of the best dishes with exceptional customer service. With that being said, here are some of the best restaurants in Karen:

1. Dari restaurant

Dari is located right in the middle of Karen, making it very convenient. It is one of the few families owned restaurants that offer local and international cuisines.

You will get to enjoy your meal surrounded by nature's flora, peacocks and flamingoes in the water. The grounds provide the perfect scenery for events or celebrations of any kind, so it's safe to drag family.

2. Tamarind Hotel

Tamarind is one of the best seafood joints in Kenya, and the hotel recently opened a branch in the Karen Blixen coffee garden. Their menu offers a variety of seafood and popular meat dishes from a variety of coastal communities. It has a quiet ambience perfect for dates or a Sunday brunch as you enjoy tunes from some of the most popular Kenyan vocalists.

3. Talisman Restaurant

Talisman Restaurant used to be some photographer's house, who recently rebuilt it into a restaurant that offers a homey vibe. The staff prides itself on its ability to provide an exceptional dining experience that will stick with you for days to come. If you prefer outdoors, they have an organic garden with beautiful scenery that works great for drinking cocktails and socializing.

For all the Indian cuisine lovers, Open House is the restaurant for you. It is one of the few Indian restaurants in Karen which always offers fresh fish and meat cooked with Indian chefs.

5. Asmara Restaurant

Asmara is an Eritrean hotel chain that specializes in integrating culture into culinary arts. Their Karen branch beautifully represents their cultural goal as their designers did not play. 6. Tanne's Tavern

This little spot has everyone clamouring for a taste, yet it is still very new. It has a lovely outdoor set up which will leave you wanting to enjoy a book drinking coffee and bagels all afternoon. If you and your boys have been on the hunt for an excellent restaurant to watch the new soccer championships, Tanne's Tavern is a definite win.

7. Picazzo Restaurant and Lounge

Picazzo is one of the few authentic Spanish restaurants in Kenya. This gem is on the first floor of the Hub Mall hence very easy to find. If you are not big on Spanish cuisines, do not shy away from the restaurant as it offers a variety of other international cuisines. Be ready to enjoy a few more glasses of wine than anticipated from their impressive collection.

8. Bedouin Restaurant

This quaint nice restaurant is located in Karen complex along Langata road. It is famous for its cheap Middle East cuisines cooked with authentic spices to maintain the taste. If you ever find yourself on Langata road and traffic is at a standstill, this is the perfect place to grab a bite as you catch the latest news on their massive screens.
