Most spectacular jewellery from the 2024 Oscars

Publish date: 2024-05-16
1

Emma Stone in Louis Vuitton

Jeff Kravitz//Getty Images

Leading Actress winner Emma Stone accepted her Oscar statue wearing a white gold, sapphire and diamond necklace from the Louis Vuitton high-jewellery collection, paired with white gold and diamond fine-jewellery earrings by the French fashion house.

2

Zendaya in Bulgari

Gilbert Flores//Getty Images

Zendaya styled her pink Armani gown with co-ordinating Bulgari high-jewellery earrings in pink gold with morganites, a pink gold and diamond bracelet, and a high-jewellery ring.

3

Jennifer Lawrence in Swarovski

Mike Coppola//Getty Images

Jennifer Lawrence debuted Swarovski's new lab-grown diamond collection on the red carpet, pairing her polka-dot Dior gown with the simple-but-effective 'Galaxy' tennis necklace and matching bracelet.

Ariana Grande in Tiffany & Co

Lupita Nyong'o in De Beers

Lupita Nyong'o added to her already glittering Armani gown with additional shine, courtesy of her 'Thames Path' diamond drop earrings, line bracelets and cocktail rings, all by De Beers.

Vanessa Hudgens in Chopard

Lexie Moreland//Getty Images

Vanessa Hudgens, who announced her pregnancy on the Oscars red carpet, dazzled in an 18k white-gold Chopard necklace featuring a 10.06 carat pear-shaped yellow diamond. She added a pair of 18k Fairmined-certified white gold and diamond earrings from the haute joaillerie collection.

Margot Robbie in Fred Leighton

Margot Robbie kept her styling simple for the 2024 Oscars, wearing a black Versace gown and just one piece of jewellery: a chunky gold and diamond bangle by Fred Leighton.

Michelle Yeoh in Cindy Chao

Michelle Yeoh wore the one-of-a-kind diamond Foliage earrings designed by jewellery artist Cindy Chao. The statement jewellery featured a selection of diamonds, yellow diamonds, pink sapphires and orange sapphires set in 18k white, yellow and rose gold.

Eva Longoria in Bucherer

Emily Blunt in Tiffany & Co
