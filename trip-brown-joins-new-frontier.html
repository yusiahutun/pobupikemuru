Trip Brown Joins New Frontier

Publish date: 2024-10-02

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) — Veteran talent rep Trip Brown has relocated to Nashville and signed on with New Frontier Touring as a Senior Agent.

"We are thrilled that Trip has joined the team at New Frontier" said president Paul Lohr, "and we welcome his impeccable taste in music and the years of experience that he brings to the table."

Brown's previous experience includes a long run as an agent at Paradigm, joining the agency in 2004. He's also done stints as an agent at ICM and William Morris, and got his start in the industry at DMA (Diversified Management Agency) in Detroit.

Brown can be reached at: tbrown (at) newfrontiertouring.com or via telephone at 615-321-6152.
