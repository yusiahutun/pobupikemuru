The Hard Rock Heals Foundation Announces Grant Recipients For 2024

Publish date: 2024-09-05

HOLLYWOOD, Fla., (CelebrityAccess) — Hard Rock Heals Foundation, the philanthropic arm of Hard Rock International hospitality brand, announced plans to contribute a total of $250,000 to charity organizations in markets where Hard Rock operates.

The grants will go to support organizations that assist with health and wellness, disaster relief, humanitarian causes, helping to improve the resiliency of the local communities.

The 2024 grant recipients include organizations such as Redefining Refuge (Tampa, Fla.), Best Point Education and Behavioral Health (Cincinnati, Ohio), Make-A-Wish Foundation of Georgia (Cobb County, Ga.), New York Edge (New York, N.Y.), Fundación Musicoterapia y Salud (Madrid, Spain) and more.

Each organization will receive a grant of $5,000, according to Hard Rock International.

“Since 2016, Hard Rock Heals Foundation has proudly supported hundreds of programs to give back to people in need in the communities we serve,” said Jon Lucas, President of Hard Rock Heals Foundation and Chief Operating Officer of Hard Rock International. “We are hopeful that these grants will bring about positive transformations in all the places we serve around the globe.”

To see the full list of grant winners, and for more information on Hard Rock Heals Foundation, visit https://www.hardrock.com/heals-foundation.aspx.
