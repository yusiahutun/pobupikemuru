Jamie Lynn Spears' daughter Maddie and father hold graveside tribute to singer's grandmother

Publish date: 2024-03-26

The holidays are a time for traditions even if they are bittersweet ones.

Jamie Lynn Spears has shared an intimate look at one of her family's most scared annual traditions.

The 24-year-old country singer posted an Instagram on Sunday of her daughter Maddie and her father Jamie tending to the grave of the star's grandmother.

Scroll down for video 

Special and somber: Jamie Lynn Spear's daughter Maddie visited the grave of her late grandmother with father Jamie on Sunday

In the touching video, the seven-year-old helps arrange flowers with her grandfather to be placed on either side of her great grandmother Lexie Pierce's headstone. 

The beloved mom and grandmother ran a restaurant before her death on December 12, 2001.

Since Lexie's death, Jamie Lynn and her father have made sure to visit her grandmother's grave - whose tombstone is engraved granny before her real name - and now little Maddie is continuing to ensure Lexie is remembered.

Letting fans in: The country star (pictured June) wanted to show an important tradition in the Spears household

Jamie Lynn captioned the video: 'Traditions with Daddy. Same place. Same Thing. Same Camo-pants. Every Year.'

The video was made even more emotional as the singing star added the Miranda Lambert track The House That Me Built, which pulls at the heartstrings just on its own.

Lexie - who is also Britney Spears' grandmother - passed away way before little Maddie was even born but clearly her mother's and grandfather's love and fond memories for the late 81-year-old mean her memory lives on.
