Career

Leland worked for his father’s bail bonds business, Da Kine Bail Bonds. Many of his relatives participated in and worked at the company, making it essentially a family venture. While there, he made the decision to go bounty hunting, mostly for the reward money.

He left Mexico and never returned.

He and his siblings then co-founded the boastful website “Chapbros Media.” After Duane went to Florida, he continued to run his father’s bail bond company and the bounty hunters in Hawaii, where he started his own bail bond business in 2012 called “Kama’aina Bail Bonds.”

In “Dog and Beth: On the Hunt,” he made a comeback. But he became a well-known showbiz personality in the US after becoming a star of the A&E Network program “Dog the Bounty Hunter.” In 2015, he and his wife Jaime relocated to Alabama, where he is still active in the bail bonds industry.
