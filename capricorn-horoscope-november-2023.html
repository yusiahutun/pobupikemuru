Capricorn Horoscope November 2023: Networking in Style

Publish date: 2024-06-28

Your Capricorn horoscope for November 2023 is all about building camaraderie and getting your networking on—as Scorpio season lights up your eleventh house of groups and community for the next few weeks, and go-getter Mars lights a fire under your social life. Making meaningful connections comes naturally now, so find ways to feel like part of a larger cause.

Your ruling planet Saturn wraps up its retrograde in your communication sector on November 4. You’re learning to speak your mind with more authority and understand the weight of your words, and this direct station will help you step up your communication game. A few days later, diplomatic Venus hits your tenth house of career, sweetening up your working relationships and making your professional accomplishments look especially sparkly to outside eyes. Now’s a great time to schmooze with the right people and make a favorable impression.

Speaking of schmoozing, you may have a surprise opportunity to share your long-term visions with like-minded people under the new moon in Scorpio on November 13, so expect the unexpected and keep an open mind. You can plant seeds for positive new friendships that align with your values and support your greater goals. The Mars cazimi on November 18 is a great time to take initiative amongst your colleagues or social crew and plan something that brings you closer together.

By the time Sagittarius season begins on November 22, you might start craving more alone time to help you recover from all the social activity. Embrace this seasonal slow-down! You’ve had a busy month, and the winter holidays are just around the corner—so reserving your energy and recharging your batteries is the right move. If you need to switch some things around in your schedule to make more space for rest, the full moon in Gemini on November 27 will shine a light on your daily responsibilities and help you reprioritize. A full moon T-square to your cosmic ruler Saturn encourages you to stand up your wellness and embrace the power of saying “no.” Self-care is a necessity, too, Capricorn.
