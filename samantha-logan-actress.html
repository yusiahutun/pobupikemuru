Samantha Jade Logan (born October 27, 1996) is an American actress. She is best known for her lead role as Olivia Baker in The CW series All American, Nina Jones in the second season of the Netflix series 13 Reasons Why, and Tia Stephens in the Freeform series The Fosters. Her other notable roles include Teen Wolf, Melissa & Joey, General Hospital and 666 Park Avenue.
