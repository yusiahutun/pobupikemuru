Kenya's media industry diversity is what the audience celebrates. Regardless of genre, language or theme preferences, one can get a radio station tweaked to their unique needs. Kigooco is a renowned media company offering unique and differentiated gospel music. And one of the most interesting things about the radio station is their presenters. Kigoco FM presenters

Without a doubt, a radio station is as good as its presenters. These uniquely talented personalities create an enticing aura by striking a conversation on any topic. And Kigooco radio station has a fantastic group of presenters that host their popular shows, from as early as 4:00 am.

Kwiiturura Hari Ngai na Mahoya with Tabitha James

Kwiiturura Hari Ngai na Mahoya is the first show at Kigoco FM, and it is all about praying and giving thanks. The idea behind the show is to refresh and recharge the soul for a good and blessed day. Tabitha James is the host, and she never disappoints. It airs from 4:00 am to 6:00 am.

Tabitha James is a born-again Christian that administers the word of God through songs. She has an album titled Kaba Gwetererea, which means wait upon God. She is currently working on her second, third, and fourth albums.

The talented radio presenter is active on social media, especially Facebook, whereby she shares her testimony and significant moments in her life. For instance, she has once shared how she narrowly escaped with her life after an attack by thugs. Also, Tabitha shared the heartbreaking story of losing her aunt.

Irathiro ria Kigooco with Wambui Wa Njihia

Irathiro ria Kigooco runs from 6:00 am to 11:00 am. It is one of the most significant shows since people wake up listening to and transition them to an hour to midday.

The show begins with an hour of prayer, worship, and a word of encouragement. Later, the host presents the day’s sermon to the audience that touches on current and trending matters.

The most interesting part of the show starts at 8:00 am and goes for one hour and thirty minutes until 9:30 am. Here, the host, Wambui Wa Wanjihia, hosts different guests. The segment is one of the most touching as Wambui talks to guests who have undergone the most difficult situations in life.

The show finalizes with half an hour of paid interviews. Also, Wambui interacts with fans during this integral session by playing requests and recognizing men of God and churches where they fellowship.

Kuriruka with Madam Charity

The Kuriruka show on Kigooco FM runs from 11:00 am to 2:00 pm and comes immediately after Wambui Wa Njihia’s morning show. The show doesn't air on Saturdays and Fridays; it is from 12:00 pm to 2:00 pm.

Madam Charity's show is one of the most engaging for Kigooco FM as she plays popular songs the audience can sing along to. However, this segment only lasts an hour. Later, between 1:00 pm and 2:00 pm, the host plays only praise and worship songs.

Madam Charity doesn't need an introduction as she has established a fabulous presence in the media industry with an excellent command of her audience. She ordinarily entertains listeners for only two hours, which many have confessed is worth it.

Matata ma Kigooco with Wakarura Wa Nyutu

Matata ma Kigooco starts the afternoon session that runs from 2:00 pm to 5:00 pm. The show is divided into segments depending on the day. It begins with a prayer session from the listeners that converse with the audience via a call line. Only one person is allowed to call and lead in prayers.

Since the radio station is gospel-oriented, the audience is encouraged with touching words from the Bible or an inspiring story from the Holy Book.

If you tune in to the radio station on Mondays during the Matata ma Kigooco show, you will be entertained by praise and worship songs mixed by the famous DJ Cent. Wakarura Wa Nyutu ends with the show with fifteen-minute preaching from Bishop Jane Kingori.

Wakarura Wa Nyutu is renowned for her Kigooco FM presenting role. She is also a host at Mt. Kenya TV, a popular television station in Kenya. Wakarura also sings gospel music, is an entrepreneur, minister, mother, and mentor.

Kuinikiriria with Muna Njomo

The Kuinikiriria show on Kigooco FM runs from 5:00 pm to 9:00 pm and is hosted by Muna Njomo. The segment starting from 5:00 pm begins with a quote to encourage listeners. And since it rush-hour show, the host gives traffic updates on major roads in Kenya. There is also a segment for debates followed by Song of the Program, popularly referred to as Mururi wa Kuinikiriria.

The second part of the show that runs from 6:00 pm to 7:00 pm is about new songs. Again, the audience is given a chance to choose what they want to listen to through SMS, phone calls, social media, and more.
