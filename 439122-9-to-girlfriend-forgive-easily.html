9 things to say to get your girlfriend to forgive you easily

Publish date: 2024-04-13

Did you do something horrible to your girlfriend, and now you are wondering whether she will break up with you? You've probably been trying to say sorry to your girlfriend, but it doesn't seem to work because she is deeply hurt. Things to say to get your girlfriend to forgive you should come in handy if you are stuck on what to say.

Saying sorry to your girlfriend can seem like the hardest thing in the world. You might find it challenging because you feel so embarrassed and ashamed of your wrong-doings, or you do not want to come down your high horse and apologize. But sometimes, you have to swallow your pride and apologize to your girlfriend if you do not want to lose her.

Men make mistakes in relationships and often lose their girlfriends just because they don't have the appetite to say sorry. Women don't forgive as easily as men do. However, if your sorry messages are genuine and sincere, then you have the chance to win her back. Here are some of the ways to apologize to your girlfriend.

1. Sorry for hurting you

The best way to amend the relationship with your girlfriend is to say sorry for causing her pain. However, if you do not know how to ask for forgiveness from your girlfriend, here are some apology sayings and messages that will help you win her heart back.

  • Please, forgive me for sounding and being so rude to you. Sorry for causing you pain. Please, find a place in your heart to forgive me.
  • I can't believe what a fool I was to treat you the way I did. I hope you can find it in your heart to forgive me and allow me to show you the kind of love and respect you truly deserve.
  • I was prone to making dumb mistakes, but I never really cared. But breaking your heart is something I cannot accept. I am terribly sorry.
  • For all the times you have not slept well, and you cried because of me, I apologize for all those sleepless nights. Please forgive me. I love you.
  • Being with you has been the best thing I have ever experienced in my life. I do not want it to end like this. Please forgive me for my stupidity; I love you.
  • Before you take the step of leaving for good, please rethink. It's better to forgive and be happy rather than otherwise.

2. I promise it won't happen again

If you want to say sorry to your girlfriend, don't start with a simple I'm sorry. Instead, start writing about the incident and promise her that it will not happen again. Then, you can end the sentence with your sincere, heartfelt sorry, as shown below.

  • Please, I've learned from my mistake. I won't make this kind of mistake next time. I beg you, forgive me. I can't wait to have you back, my pretty damsel.
  • I promise you that I will be a better person from now on, and I will never hurt you again. I am sorry.
  • Your smile makes my heart. Please allow me to be the reason for your smile. I am sorry, I will never do it again.

3. I take responsibility for my actions

How far are you willing to go to get back with your girlfriend? Understanding what went wrong will be one of the first things you need to know and be ready to admit to. So, here are some messages that will prove that you are taking responsibility for every action or deed.

  • I messed up. I had the most beautiful relationship of my life, and I threw it away for a pipedream. Now, I've come to my senses and realized how wrong I was. Please forgive me!
  • I did not mean any of the harsh words I said earlier. I am sorry for being immature. Please forgive me.

4. I promise that I will change

Forgiveness does not change the past, but it does enlarge the future. Therefore, you need to show you are willing to change and be a better person for the sake of the future. Use these simple phrases to show your willingness to change.

  • Sweetheart, I'm turning over a new leaf. I am so sorry for the failure of a boyfriend I've been in the past. Please forgive me!
  • I promise that this mistake has taught me how to be a better person. Please give me the chance to show you.
  • I promise I will destroy every reason that brings tears to your eyes. Please forgive me this time. I am sorry.
  • I am constantly and deeply ashamed of what I have done. I hope that you will let me show you how I have changed. I'm sorry!
  • I promise you that I will put more effort into our relationship so that it goes a long way. I am sorry, please stay with me. I love you.
  • I will make up for all the days I ruined. Please forgive me. I am sorry.
  • I have truly repented for all the things I've done, and I want to say I am truly sorry, and I love you more than you can ever imagine. If you choose to forgive me, then I promise I will be different and make your life, no, our lives, better. I love you.

5. You mean so much to me

When she means everything to you, it means your heart has been completely captured, and there's no point hiding the feeling. So open your heart and tell her that she means so much to you using these simple phrases.

  • No matter how many times you refuse to forgive me, I will keep trying. Because my love for you is something that cannot be easily forgotten.
  • If I atone hard for years, I cannot find someone so honest as you. Please don't create distance between you and me. I love you.
  • In this brutal world, I shall not be able to survive without you. Please do not abandon me. Extremely sorry for everything.
  • I am very fortunate to meet someone like you. Please don't go away; you are my life. I am sorry for my wrong actions.
  • You know that I cannot find anyone with such beautiful eyes as you. Please stay with me. I am sorry, Beautiful.

6. Give me a second chance

Life is all about second chances. Second chances mean having that one more opportunity to get closer to your girlfriend. You can ask her to give you a second chance so that you can win her affection. Check out these messages for inspiration.

  • They say that everyone deserves a second chance. So I am begging you to give me one. I know that what I did was not right, but that is no reason for this beautiful bond we share to break like this. Please forgive me; I love you.
  • You mean the world to me. Please give me one more chance to prove my love to you. I am sorry. I love you.
  • Even though I don't deserve it, would you ever be able to find it in your heart to forgive me? I want another chance to win your affection.
  • If I could go back in time and change how I've treated you in the past, I would. I can't, but I'd love a chance to show you how much I've changed in the future. Is there any way you could forgive me and give me that chance?

7. I cannot live without you

If you love your girlfriend so much, you cannot imagine life without her. You need her to forgive you so that you can hold her in your arms again. If you cannot find the right words to use, the quotes below should come in handy.

  • I know that my actions have damaged the bond that the two of us share, but I am asking you to forgive me because I cannot live without you beside me. I am sorry.
  • I will not be able to survive without you in this cruel world. Please don't leave me. I am sorry for everything.
  • Being separated from you is my version of living hell. I cannot stand your absence anymore. I am begging for forgiveness. Please return to me; I am sorry.
  • No matter how many distractions I try to give myself, your absence is something I cannot handle. My world is empty without you. Please forgive me and come back to my arms. I love you.
  • Losing you has shown me how empty life is without you. I want to be with you again. I am so sorry for hurting you and pushing you away. Will you accept my apology?

8. I regret everything I have done

Girls won't forgive anyone easily. It will take some effort to make her happy again and forget your mistake. If you want to say sorry to your girlfriend, tell her that you regret everything you have done to her. Here are sample regret messages you can send to her.

  • After all the harm I've caused, I'm here to tell you I regret and I'm genuinely sorry, my love.
  • The realization of what I have done cuts deeply into my soul and stabs my heart. I cannot justify my actions, but I regret them every moment and ask you to give me another chance.
  • I cannot believe that I managed to break the heart of such a beautiful creature like you. I am a giant fool for not being more careful of my words and actions. I am terribly sorry. I regret everything I've done. Please forgive me; I love you.

9. I miss you

It has been said that the way to a woman's heart is through words. Sending missing you messages for girlfriend is a sure way to win her heart over and over. Below are miss-you messages that will help you win her back.

  • My hand misses the feel of holding yours. My eyes have nothing beautiful to look at without you. My ears miss being filled with the sound of your sweet voice. My heart only beats because it hopes for your forgiveness.
  • Leaving you was the biggest mistake of my life. I miss everything about you. Is there any way you could find it in your heart to forgive me and take me back?
  • The beauty of your face and the kindness of your heart fills my thoughts by day and my dreams by night. I miss you. Please forgive me.
