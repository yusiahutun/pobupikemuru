Goddess of... bingo? Fiery redhead Helen Flanagan sprouts angel wings for ad

Publish date: 2024-07-02

But that hasn't stopped her from sprouting a pair of wings to morph into a butter-wouldn't-melt angel.

The boobylicious actress has joined bingo website Bingo Godz as a guest god, and has shed her asset-flashing vixen persona for alter ego Helena the Mancunian Goddess of Love.

We don't remember seeing that particular legend in Greek mythology, but hey, whatever floats your boat.

The 23-year-old showcased her fiery new red locks in the site promos, and looked positively angelic as she wore a leafy garland in her curls.

Donning an off-white dress that – gasp! – concealed her cleavage, Helen looks shyly at the ground while wearing a pair of feathery angel wings.

She accessorised her godly look with gold earrings, several bangles and a jewelled pendant – but it wasn't just her auburn tresses that stole attention.

The Marilyn Monroe tattoo on Helen's forearm is facing forward in her solo portrait, and completely stole the focus away from the rest of her saintly look.

The former Corrie actress joined two other bingo gods – Cleopatra and Zeus – for a few group shots, with one showing off her pins in a pair of strapped gold heels.

Helen then took to bed with her new pals for a godly threesome, flashing her pearly white smile for the camera before striking a wide-eyed pout face.

Helen's gorgeous goddess will be available in cartoon form as an avatar for customers on the site.

The star said: "I love my character! Who wouldn’t want to be immortalised into their very own godly alter-ego.

"It was a lot of fun on the photo-shoot and I hope that my appearance brings players lots of luck. Who would have thought there would be a god from Manchester?"

We're as surprised as you are.
