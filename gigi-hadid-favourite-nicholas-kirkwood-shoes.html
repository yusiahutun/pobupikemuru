Gigi Hadids favourite 315 summer shoes from Nicholas Kirkwood

Publish date: 2024-03-19

The Victoria’s Secret model can’t get enough of these nude-toned backless mules from British shoe designer Nicholas Kirkwood

    Gigi Hadid’s current go-to shoes are just the thing for early summer. The model can’t get enough of the Beya flat mules from British shoe designer Nicholas Kirkwood. Effortlessly elegant, the backless soft leather shoes are priced at £315 and feature a pointed toe and an ultra-low heel.

    The star adores her Nicholas Kirkwood leather loafers

    At the beginning of the month she wore her favourite shoes for an evening out in the Big Apple. The American beauty worked a low-key ensemble with her sliders – opting for a pair of cropped white jeans and a striped blouse. 

    STORY: Gigi works a casual look while out in NYC with Kendall Jenner

    A few days earlier the 22-year-old wore the Nicholas Kirkwood mules again while out and about strolling in New York. She teamed them with an all-yellow ensemble that consisted of a pair of high-waisted trousers, a crop top and a sweater that she tied around her shoulders.

    The nude Nicholas Kirkwood 'Beya' loafers can be purchased from Farfetch for £315

    Though perfect for the warmer weather, Gigi was wearing the backless shoes back in January, proving that they’re a go-to style for winter days too. She looked casually cool in a long olive coat, pale denim, white button-down tee and aviator sunglasses.

    Stylish without being too out-there, backless loafers are the perfect in-between shoe. They can be teamed with a loose, ankle-skimming dress and a slouchy blazer for everyday chic or rocked with ripped jeans and a slogan T-shirt for an edgier look.

    The Victoria's Secret model looked cool and casual as she meandered around New York with Kendall Jenner

    Gigi looked worked her signature off-duty model look a couple of weeks back as she stepped out in New York with her friend Kendall Jenner. The Victoria’s Secret Angel looked casual in a black form-fitting crop top teamed with a pair of skinny jeans and Tommy Hilfiger iconic briefs worn high on her waist.
