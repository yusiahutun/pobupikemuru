Great for Blue Eyes

Charlotte Tilbury Pillow Talk Push Up Lashes Volumizing & Lengthening Mascara

Pros
  • Lifting and Volumizing

Customer Review:

"Okay so first off the color is stunning. I have blue eyes and it is seriously the most beautiful mascara I’ve owned, I don’t ever want to go back to standard black mascara. I will say the first few applications were a little frustrating. I found it to be heavy and didn’t hold a curl at all, I wasn’t impressed. However, I tinkered with it and found that if I start at the tips of my eyelashes and kind of work my way backwards to the base of my lashes, almost combing as I go, it applied beautifully. And holds a curl, separated, and doesn’t smudge. I love it now that I know how to apply it the right way for me." -Kat Swan
