Christi Paul Bio, Wiki

Christi was born on 1st January 1969 in Hong Kong. She was born to Rojan Donald and Ann Elizabeth. She holds an American nationality and belongs to white ethnicity. Likewise, her birth sign is Capricorn. Similarly, Christi grew up in  Bellevue, Ohio, the United States.

Paul attended local high school in Bellevue and secured a bachelor’s degree of Broadcast Journalism from the University of Toledo. She is passionate about modeling since her early age as she holds the title of former Miss Mansfield, Ohio, Miss Greater Cleveland in 1991-1992 and the third runner-up as Miss Ohio in 2013.

Besides this, Paul is the author of a book entitled “Love Isn’t Supposed to Hurt” published in 2012.
