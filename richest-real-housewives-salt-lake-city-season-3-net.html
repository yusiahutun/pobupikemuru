While the cast’s estimated net worths have been widely reported, due to the shocking events of season two, one can’t help but wonder what – if anything – has changed with the glamorous residents of Utah’s snow-capped capital.

Of the five full-time RHOSLC cast members, the major net worth shake-up would surely be with lawsuit-embattled Shah? Let’s take a closer look.

5. Jen Shah, 49

Estimated net worth: Unknown

Though the feisty reality star was previously reported to be worth US$3 million, per Screenrant, due to the scandalous events of the past couple of months, Shah’s personal fortune has presumably deteriorated substantially.

This is in light of damning accusations that the RHOSLC star was intricately involved in major money wire and telemarketing schemes that defrauded older, vulnerable people. Shah initially maintained her innocence and denied all allegations of wrongdoing in relation to what the courts, per Us Weekly, referred to as a US$5 million money laundering scheme.

During her first court hearing, Shah was set a “recognisance bond [bail] of US$1 million”, US$250,000 of which was ordered to be paid within a week. It was during this hearing that the reality star’s lawyer admitted to the courts that she owns no real assets, including the massive home viewers saw on the show, per US Weekly

Following a series of further legal proceedings, including failed attempts to have the case dismissed, Shah finally admitted guilt, changing her plea in court in July. As a result, per Deadline, Shah is currently facing a possible sentence of as much as 14 years behind bars as well as restitution of up to US$9.5 million.

Considering the hefty bail coupled with the undoubtedly costly legal fees incurred throughout the course of the legal drama, Shah’s net worth is now murkier than ever, with no reliable estimates recently reported.

So what will ultimately become of the RHOSLC’s most polarising star? More will be revealed after her sentencing, which is scheduled for November 28 this year.

4. Whitney Rose, 36

Estimated net worth: US$1 million

Though initial estimates had put Whitney Rose’s personal net worth at some US$3 million, somewhere around US$1 million is a more realistic estimation of the Salt Lake City stunner’s personal fortune, per both Realhousewives.net and Full Celebs.

Rose owns and operates her own skincare line, Wild Rose Beauty – but as viewers saw in season two, the entrepreneur admitted to using a large portion of her family’s savings to further develop her company.

3. Meredith Marks, 50

Estimated net worth: US$1 million

Per Distractify, Meredith Marks boasts an estimated bank balance of US$1 million. The bulk of these rumoured riches derives from her successful, eponymous high-end jewellery and fashion accessory company. Marks’ pieces have been seen adorning some of the world’s most famous faces, including Rihanna and Charlize Theron.

Besides the ostensibly thriving Meredith Marks online business, which per the company’s website is expanding its offerings with handbags, masks and even coffee, the chic RHOSLC star also has a bricks-and-mortar boutique store located in Utah.

2. Heather Gay, 40

Estimated net worth: US$1.7 Million

As the show’s second wealthiest star in 2022, per British media, Heather Gay’s reported net worth is around US$1.7 million.

The primary source of the outspoken, once devout Mormon’s money is likely to come from Beauty Lab + Laser, the beauty and wellness spa she co-owns and operates with her business partner Andrea Robinson.

Gay has also written a memoir, Bad Mormon, currently available for pre-order, with expected publication in February 2023.

1. Lisa Barlow, 47

Estimated net worth: US$5 million

As reported by CA Knowledge, self-proclaimed tequila queen Lisa Barlow remains the richest RHOSLC cast member. Her estimated net worth is still said to be around US$5 million.
