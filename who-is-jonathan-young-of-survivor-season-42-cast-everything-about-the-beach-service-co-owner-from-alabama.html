Survivor Cast Jonathan Young Wikipedia Details

Jonathan Young is one of the contestants in the show, Survivor 42 season. He is 29 years old from Gadsden, Alabama and, he is currently residing on Gulf shores. But he is yet to have his own Wikipedia page.

When he was 14 years old he participated in the show called Endurance which was like a Survivor show for kids. The show didn’t use to starve them as they were kids but, he got to know through the show what Survivor would be like.
