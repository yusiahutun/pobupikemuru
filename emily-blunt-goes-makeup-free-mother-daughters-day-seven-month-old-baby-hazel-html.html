She's in a popular meditation hot spot, so it only makes sense to relax.

And that's just what Emily Blunt appeared to be doing, as she was spotted strolling down the street in the quiet Southern Californian town with her seven-month-old baby girl Hazel.

The 31-year-old Looper actress was completely makeup free as she enjoyed a mother and daughter's day out, apparently without her husband John Krasinski.

Baby's day out: A stunning and makeup-free Emily Blunt strolled she and her husband John Krasinski's baby girl Hazel around Los Angeles on Thursday looking chic and casual in a jumpsuit

Emily kept things comfortable in a dark green patterned jumpsuit featuring long trousers and a tank style upper half, with the loose-fitting fabric catering for the summery temperatures.

She wore silver flip flops and shielded her eyes from the sun with aviator style sunglasses.

When she removed her shades, it was clear that it was a makeup free day for the star, who also wore her long brunette tresses out and naturally flowing around her shoulders.

And up: The 31-year-old Looper actress picked up her little one, who is seven months old, for a cuddle

Hello, world! The adorable little girl, who was born to the pair of actors on February 16

The Devil Wears Prada stunner initially had her baby girl in a pram, but later picked up the adorable tot for a cuddle.

Hazel is seven-months old; Emily and John welcomed her into the world on February 16.

She is the very first child for the couple, who were married in Como, Italy in July of 2010.

Jetset already: On Sunday, Emily and Hazel were seen shortly after touching down in New York City

Casual flyer: The beautiful actress was dressed comfortably for the flight as she donned a knit sweater, dark grey sweatpants and brown suede Converse sneakers

Emily and John in fact own a $2 million house on five acres in Ojai, which is two hours outside of Los Angeles, and they use the place as a weekender.

Back in June, Emily told Ellen DeGeneres how much she dotes on her new daughter.

'She's just stinking cute. Yeah she's so cute. She came out, like, ready for a photo shoot,' Emily gushed.

'She was like this, just perfect.'

Adorable: Hazel was draped in all pink as she was attached to her famous mother's body via a baby carrier

The British actress also gushed over her Emmy-nominated husband, whom she calls 'the burp king' and 'the greatest dad'.

On the job front, Emily just finished shooting the crime thriller Sicario in sweltering New Mexico.

Josh Brolin, Benicio Del Toro and The Walking Dead's Jon Bernthal also star in the 2015 flick helmed by Denis Villeneuve (Prisoners).
