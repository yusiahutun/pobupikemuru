Why Is Amanda Batula Not on Winter House Season 3? Where Is She?

Publish date: 2024-08-01

Ever since she was missing from the first episode, viewers have been wondering why Amanda Batula isn’t on Winter House Season 3 and where she is from the Bravo spin-off.

Amanda’s husband, Kyle Cooke, is a cast member on Season 3 of Winter House, Bravo’s reality TV series following a group of Bravo stars from various shows as they vacation in the snow. The series, which is a spin-off of Bravo’s Summer House set in The Hamptons, New York, premiered in 2021 and has since featured more than a dozen Bravo stars from across shows like Southern Charm and Below Deck.

The Winter House cast for Season 3 includes eight main cast members from six Bravo shows: Vanderpump Rules, Summer House, Below Deck Mediterranean, Below Deck Sailing Yacht, Summer House: Martha’s Vineyard, and Family Karma. According to Entertainment Tonight, Vanderpump Rules star Tom Sandoval was set to be a part of the Winter House cast for Season 3, however, he was cut after news broke of his affair—now known as Scandoval—involving his ex-girlfriend, Ariana Madix, and their co-star, Raquel Leviss. The site also reported that Tom Schwartz, Sandoval’s Vanderpump Rules co-star, also almost missed Winter House filming due to Scandoval, which required cameras to pick back up for Vanderpump Rules Season 10.

Amanda starred in Winter House Seasons 1 and 2, but where is she in Season 3. Why isn’t Amanda Batula on Winter House Season 3 with Kyle? Read on for why Amanda Batula isn’t on Winter House Season 3 and if she’s ever arriving to the cabin.

  • Why is Amanda Batula not on Winter House Season 3?

    Why is Amanda Batula not on Winter House Season 3? Amanda, who was on Winter House Seasons 1 and 2, was missing from the premiere of Season 3 because she was at a bachelorette party. “Amanda! What’s up, how was the bachelorette?” Amanda’s husband, Kyle Cooke, asked her on a FaceTime call in the premiere. “I’m very happy in my element.” Kyle then told Amanda that he missed her, to which Amanda responded, “I don’t. I mean, I love you. I don’t feel like I miss you just yet.”

    In a confessional interview, Kyle explained that he wished Amanda was with him on Winter House Season 3, but he understood why she needed a break. “I love Amanda. I wish she was here, but at the same time, I am a lot for her to handle. For a couple days, I can let my freak flag fy since no one is there to chop it down.”

    Amanda confirmed on her call with Kyle that she’ll arrive to the winter house in “a few days.” However, her arrival seemed to be delayed even further after Amanda came down with a “sudden illness” and couldn’t travel. “Despite being the only married man in the house, Kyle Cooke (Summer House) still loves to party with his single friends, and hopes to watch sparks fly between them. He is looking forward to getting some work-free time to bond with his wife, Amanda Batula (Summer House), but after a sudden illness delays her arrival out West, an even more unexpected situation comes their way,” reads Kyle and Amanda’s official biography for Winter House Season 3 on Bravo’s website.

    However, despite the delays, Amanda is a full-time cast member on Winter House Season 3. Amanda also confirmed she was a cast member on Winter House Season 3 in an Instagram post in September 2023.

    “WinterHouse Season 3 premieres October 24th on @BravoTV!” she captioned her official cast photo for the season.
