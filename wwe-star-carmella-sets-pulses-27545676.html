WWE star Carmella sets pulses racing as she shares bikini snaps of her 'great a**'

Publish date: 2024-02-25

WWE star Carmella - real name Leah Van Dale - has wowed fans with a series of sultry bikini snaps.

The American wrestler, dancer and model was seen posing in a cheeky red two piece on a sunny beach in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Sharing six sizzling snaps with her 3.3million Instagram followers, she wrote: "A badass with a great a**."

One fan jokingly warned "watch out for the pirates" while posting a zoomed in crop of the ship that appears in the water behind her in one snap.

Another gushed: "Definitely!! Let em know, Mella!!"

Some wrestling fans pointed out that the "badass with a great a**" caption was first used by another wrestler and model, Natalia Markova.

"That @RealNMarkova‘s line. How unoriginal of you," one fumed.

Natalia herself got involved, replying to that fan: "Yes, @CarmellaWWE stole my catchphrase."

"@RealNMarkova about to hit you with that cease and desist," joked another fan.

"'She stole @RealNMarkova caption' the f**k who cares," someone else added.

Carmella first signed with WWE in June 2013, making her main roster debut on SmackDown in July 2016.

The following year she claimed her first major title, winning the inaugural women's Money in the Bank ladder match.

In April 2018, she became the first woman in WWE history to successfully cash-in her Money in the Bank opportunity. She saw off Charlotte Flair to win the WWE SmackDown Women's Championship.

Carmella went on to win both the WrestleMania Women's Battle Royal and the WWE 24/7 Championship in 2019.

After that she teamed up up with Queen Zelina - with whom she won the WWE Women's Tag Team Championship - after being drafted to RAW in 2021.

In 2019, she started a relationship with former wrestler Corey Graves - real name Matthew Polinksy.

Corey is now a commentator for the WWE and the pair got married in April this year.

The couple also launched a podcast together in June 2020, Bare With Us, in which they discuss issues regarding relationships including ones within their own.
