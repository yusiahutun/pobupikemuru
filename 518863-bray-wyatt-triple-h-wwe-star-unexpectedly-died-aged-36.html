Bray Wyatt: 7 Photos of WWE Star As He Unexpectedly Died Aged 36

Publish date: 2024-04-29
  • WWE fans are in mourning following the death of Windham Rotunda, better known as Bray Wyatt
  • Wyatt's father, WWE legend Mike Rotunda confirmed the news of his demise and announced by Triple H
  • Tribute messages poured in for the 36-year-old who won three world championships, including the WWE Championship and Universal Championship

WWE superstar Bray Wyatt is dead.

News of the 36-year-old's unexpected death was announced by 14-time world champion and Chief Content Officer Triple H.

How did Bray Wyatt die?

According to Triple H, he received a call from Wyatt’s father, a former wrestler, confirming his death on Thursday, August 24.

Wyatt also had an alter ego called The Fiend, and his signature move was Sister Abigail.
