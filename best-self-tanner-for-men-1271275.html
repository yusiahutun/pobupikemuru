    How to Choose the Best Self-Tanner for Men?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best self-tanner for men: 

    Skin Type And Undertone: Ensure that you analyze your skin type and undertone before splurging on any of the best self-tanners for men. It is wise to invest in the one that not only helps you attain the perfect tan but also leaves your skin utterly nourished. If you have sensitive skin, then choose a tanner that is gentle and mild just like St.Tropez Self Tan Classic Bronzing Mousse. On the other hand, pay heed to your skin undertones to achieve a desirable shade. 

    • Warm Undertone: For all those who possess a warm undertone, make sure you get your hands on a good-quality men's self-tanner that features a lighter and warmer shade. 
    • Cool Undertone: For those who have cool undertone skin, pick the best men's self-tanner that is darker in shade.

    To accentuate the beauty of your existing skin tone, choose a tanning cream for men that matches your natural skin color. Likewise, to attain a deep and darker tan, pick a buildable tanning lotion for men that comes in an ultra-dark shade so that you can easily attain the color you desire. 

    Type of Self-tanner: There are 4 types of self-tanners available on the market. They differ according to their respective texture, formula, application method, and consistency. Here are some common types:

    • Mousse: Self-tanning mousse is a type of bronzed foam. It is widely used by those who possess dry skin. 
    • Lotion: A self-tanning lotion is easily buildable and it works for all skin types. 
    • Spray: A self-tanning spray is nothing but a dense mist that is widely used by professionals. 

    Drops: For temporary results and everyday usage, self-tanning drops work the best.

    Ingredients: Check the list of ingredients before getting your hands on the self-tanner for men. It is advisable to invest in those that are enriched with active ingredients as well as gentle ingredients like vitamin E, aloe vera, hyaluronic acid, cocoa seed extract, coconut, botanical extract, etc just like Glossiva Illuminating Self-tan Drops. Do not invest in tanners that contain synthetic ingredients or harsh chemicals. 

    Coverage: Pay heed to the level of coverage offered by the tanner. Some of them will lend you a subtle tan whereas others might give a deeper color. This usually depends on the shade and type of tanner. So, always check the shade and level of coverage that it promises to offer.

    Formula: Next, let’s talk about the formula! The formula of the tanner is also of utmost importance. It is advisable to invest in a good-quality men's tanning moisturizer, lotion, spray, or drop formulation that is non-comedogenic, and free from parabens, phthalates, gluten, and silicone to avoid skin rashes, allergies, or inflammation. 

    Texture: Last but not least, the texture of the tanner allows you to predict its efficacy. For those who have dry skin, choose a tanner that has a creamy and thick texture whereas for those who have oily skin, you may pick a tanner that is light in weight and non-sticky just like Coco & Eve Antioxidant Face Tanning Micromist. Make sure that it doesn’t clog your pores. 

    Now, let us walk you through a few steps for using one appropriately.

    Steps to Apply Self-Tanner for a Natural-looking Tan

    Below are the steps for applying a self-tanner accurately: 

    Step 1: Exfoliate your skin to sweep away the dead skin cells and moisturize your skin thoroughly. 

    Step 2: Start applying the tanner to your feet, and then apply it to your hands and face in a circular motion. You can apply it directly with your hands or use a mitt.

    Step 3: Apply it evenly to avoid any patches or streaky results.

    Step 4: Let the tanner stay on the skin as directed. 

    Step 5: Rinse it with lukewarm water. 

    Step 6: Damp your skin gently with a microfiber towel. 

    Step 7: Apply a sunscreen.

    Tada, you are done! Now, come along to know how to remove it.

    Ways to Remove Self-Tanner

    Following are the various ways you can use to remove the self-tanner effectively:

    • Exfoliate your skin thoroughly. 
    • Apply oil and thoroughly rub it on the skin before wiping off the tan. 
    • You may try using a sugar-based body or face scrub to remove the tan with ease.
    • Use a hair removal cream. 
    • You may pour vinegar into a cotton ball and dab it onto your skin. Let it sit for two minutes. Rinse your skin with warm water.
    • Apply a whitening toothpaste onto the tanned areas and leave it on for at least 15 minutes. Rinse it with warm water and damp your skin gently.

    FAQ's

    How important is preparing your skin before tanning?

    Exfoliating will eliminate dead skin cells and unclog your pores.

    What are the common mistakes people make when applying a self-tanner?

    Some common mistakes people make when applying a self-tan is that they usually over-apply the self-tanner the first time and do not give enough time for it to dry completely.

    Will a self-tanner give an orange tint?

    A self-tanner can give an orange tint if you apply it over dry spots, they will likely look orange.

    How long does it take to see results from a self-tanner?

    A self-tanner will immediately start showing results. Usually, the tan will keep getting darker over the next 10 hours.

    Are self-tanners safe for all skin types?

    It depends from brand to brand. A few of them might not suit specific skin types like sensitive or very dry skin.

    Why should men consider using self-tanners?

    Men can consider using self-tanners to undergo a transformation and look more dapper.

    How to avoid streaks and uneven color with self-tanners?

    You can avoid streaks and uneven color by exfoliating your skin thoroughly before using a self-tanner and applying it evenly and well, following package instructions.

    Are there specific self-tanners suitable for travel?

    Yes, there are specific self-tanners that come in small sizes and facilitate mess-free applications suitable for travel.
