How to increase your Hustler Fund limit and access more money

Publish date: 2024-02-27

When the Hustler Funds was launched on November 30th, 2022, it gave many Kenyans hope. However, unknown to many of them was how to increase your Hustler Fund limit. So, how much do you know about how to access and increase the limit?

The Hustler Fund is now fully operational, and Kenyans are pretty excited. With many of them accessing and browsing funds, they are also concerned with increasing the limit. Here is how to increase your Hustler Fund limit.

What is the Hustler Fund?

The Hustler fund is a digital financial inclusion initiative. It is designed to improve financial access to responsible finance for personal, micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises.

The Hustler Fund incorporates both borrowing and savings components. President William Samoei Ruto and Cabinet Secretary Simon Chelugui launched it. You can register and access the Hustler Fund on any mobile network. Simply dial *254# or use the mobile app.

Hustler fund terms and conditions for borrowing and repayment

The requirements for you to access Hustler Fund include the following:

  • Be a Kenyan citizen (above 18 years old).
  • Have a valid National Identification card.
  • Have a registered mobile number with a recognised mobile network operator in Kenya.
  • Provide residence details.

How to apply for the Hustler Fund loan

A registered customer is required to follow the steps below:

  • Dial USSD code *USSD code# or mobile application.
  • Select the loan request option to view the limit, interest, and loan tenure.
  • Enter the loan amount and press OK to continue.
  • Confirm loan details as displayed to continue.
  • Enter your Mobile Money PIN.

    • You will receive SMS notifications on loan allocation with relevant loan information.

    Hustler loan limit

    The maximum you can borrow is Ksh 50,000, and the minimum is Ksh 500 at an 8 per cent pro-rated basis. However, it does not automatically mean everyone can access the Ksh. 50,000.

    Hustler Fund application, requirements, interest rates, and limit

    Once you submit an application, it will be subjected to the predetermined formula based on existing qualification parameters. Interested Kenyans will not need collateral, guarantors, or paperwork. It’s a digital loan product, just like M-Shwari and KCB M-Pesa.

    Hustler Fund offers better interest rates. The approval for the loan shall be granted based on meeting eligibility criteria and credit worthiness. Therefore, the Hustler Fund limit varies for different people.

    How to increase your hustler fund limit

    This is a fund for low-income earners who wish to venture into small businesses. You can start borrowing from the Fund with as low as Ksh. 500. However, with time, you can increase your limit to as high as Ksh. 50,000.

    So, how do you increase Hustler Fund quickly? If you want to increase the loan limit allocated, you can only do so through continuous borrowing and timely repayment.

    Hustler Fund loan repayment

    The loan repayment will be in fourteen days. The interest will be charged at 8% per annum. For instance, if you borrow Ksh 500, your repayment will be Ksh 501.53 after fourteen days. It is way cheaper than most digital loan products in the country.

    Additionally, repayment will be made through mobile money without any transaction costs. If you mistakenly borrow a loan, you will have to repay it.

    What happens if you default on a payment?

    When you do not repay your loan within the required timelines, you won’t be added to a Credit Reference Bureau (CRB). However, after 15 days more of lack of repayment, loan interest will go up to 9.5% per annum.

    After 30 days, if you haven’t paid, your account will be frozen. You will lose the credit score accumulated, but this doesn’t affect other loan services.
