See Kourtney Kardashian Dress Her Bump in a Sheer Orange Bodycon Dress

Publish date: 2024-04-27

Kourtney Kardashian embraces the autumnal season in her latest edgy maternity looks.

The Poosh founder posted a new photo dump on Instagram yesterday, sharing a series of snapshots from the past few months. In addition to candid images of her pumpkin home decor and selfies taken in bed with husband Travis Barker, the star included some new peeks into her recent outfits.

For one standout photo, the pregnant star leans against a stack of large Goyard suitcases while dressed in a body-hugging maxi dress made of completely see-through fabric. The bright orange piece featured long sleeves, a gray polo collar, and black-and-white striped detailing along the sides. Her black mesh triangle bralette and bottoms were visible under the dress. In a separate pic, she posed in just the underwear set while applying lip gloss in her bathroom.

Kourtney Kardashian//Instagram

Other trendy outfits highlighted in the post include a frilly gray minidress with circular ruffles and detailed ruching along the front, as well as an all-black look composed of an oversized puffer coat, a column maxi skirt, and mesh ballet flats. The expectant mother also gave non-sartorial glimpses of her pregnancy in some sweet images, including a close-up of a curved wooden crib and a shot of a bouquet of pink tulips from her daughter Penelope, which came with a handwritten note reading, "I hope you feel better Mom!"

Kardashian recently opened up about her recent health care for the first time since she confirmed that she underwent "urgent fetal surgery." She also spoke on the restrictions that came with being an expectant mother in her mid-40s.

"I think all this caution made me a little afraid because in the past I had never had to be careful," she said. "It took me a while to let go of the fear. I would say that right after the surgery I got to the point where I let go and stopped worrying. Now I talk to the child every day, have a positive mindset, keep my head straight. and say a lot of prayers. I feel really lucky and grateful."
