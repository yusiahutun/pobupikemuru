E4 Married at First Sight UK fans concerned as star mysteriously missing from finale

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Married at First Sight UK star Arthur Poremba was mysteriously missing from the series finale.

The tennis coach was left gutted during the vow renewals as his wife Laura Vaughan decided to end their marriage and they both went their separate ways. They were matched by show experts Paul C Brunson, Mel Schilling and Charlene Douglas but things didn't work out between the pair.

The reunion episodes were filmed six weeks after the final vows and Arthur attended the last dinner party which aired on Wednesday (November 15) night but he failed to show up to the commitment ceremony which on Thursday (November 16) evening. Before talking to each of the couples, Mel informed the group that both Arthur and Nathanial Valentino, who quit the process after his marriage broke down to Ella Morgan, were no present.

Arthur left viewers in hysterics during the final dinner party as he turned up wearing a vest and baggy jeans. With the show experts watching on from a separate room, Mel mused: "Arthur didn't get the dress code memo." It then cut to a visibly annoyed Laura saying: "Arthur looks like he's come straight from the gym," adding sarcastically, "I''s nice to see he's really put an effort in for seeing his ex-wife for the first time."

Undeterred by his ex-wife's scowling expression, Arthur walked over to greet her and compliment her on how beautiful she looked, with the experts praising his politeness. Arthur's unusual clothing choice comes after he was on the receiving end of an speech by Laura at the vow renewal. Arthur was left visibly shaken by Laura's words as he teared up while discussing their vows with a producer. "That made me feel like c***.
