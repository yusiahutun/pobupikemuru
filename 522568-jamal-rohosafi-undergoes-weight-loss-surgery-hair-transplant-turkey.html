Jamal Rohosafi Undergoes Weight Loss Surgery after Getting Hair Transplant in Turkey

Publish date: 2024-07-05
  • Jamal Rohosafi has opted for weight-loss surgery, and he is hoping to lose 20 kilograms in four months
  • The businessman had undergone a successful hair transplant procedure on Monday, September 25
  • Jamal has documented his journey to a healthier lifestyle while in Turkey on social media with lovely videos

Nairobi businessman Jamal Rohosafi has undergone weight loss surgery just a day after having a successful hair transplant procedure.

Jamal revealed his decision to opt for weight loss surgery in an Instagram post on Tuesday, September 26.

Jamal shedding 20 kgs for healthier life

The affluent tycoon, currently in Turkey for these surgeries, expressed his goal to shed 20kgs, which led him to choose the Allurion Intragastric Balloon procedure.

In his Instagram update, Jamal stated:

"I need to move from 95kg to 75kg in four months. Today is Allurion Intragastric Balloon."

The father of three has been updating his fans on his experience and showing them the transformation through photos and videos.

Jamal thanks doctor after hair transplant procedure

The business mogul took to Instagram to share his experience and praise the doctor as the best in the world.

He expressed his gratitude for the successful surgery and revealed that the doctor would soon be visiting Kenya and Africa.

"Best doctor in the world ranked for hair transplant. Soon, he will be in Kenya and Africa. He said I should find him a person in Kenya. Should I connect him to somebody?"
