See 'Last Man Standing' Star Kaitlyn Dever's &quot;Breathtaking&quot; Met Gala Gown

Publish date: 2024-02-10

The Met Gala was a star-studded event, with head-turning looks from celebrities like Nicole Kidman, Rihanna, and Olivia Wilde. This year's gala honored Karl Lagerfeld with the theme, "Karl Lagerfeld: A Line of Beauty," and stars honored the fashion icon's style with their looks.

Kaitlyn Dever stood out among the crowd for her bold red gown and ultra-glamorous style. The actress, who is known for her roles in Last Man Standing, Justified, Dopesick, and Booksmart, wore a head-turning gown that called to mind classic Hollywood glamour.

Jeff Kravitz

The strapless mermaid-style lace gown by Michael Kors accentuated Kaitlyn's hourglass figure. It featured side cutouts, a dramatic pooling train, and an oversize rose detail at the waist.

Sean Zanni

Kaitlyn wore her long hair in glamorous old Hollywood waves and kept jewelry minimal except for dangly diamond earrings to keep the focus on her dress. She wore bright red lipstick and a matching red manicure.

Kevin Mazur/MG23

Kaitlyn posed alongside Vanessa Hudgens, Ashley Park, Kerry Washington, and Michael Kors.

Kevin Mazur/MG23

She caught up with her former costars, Ben Platt (Dear Evan Hansen) and Phillipa Soo (Dopesick).
