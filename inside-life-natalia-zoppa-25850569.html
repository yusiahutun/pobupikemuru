Inside life of Natalia Zoppa from Love Island and OnlyFans to 'Jack Grealish DMs'

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Natalia Zoppa has enjoyed a fast track rise to fame in the past two years ever since starring in Love Island.

The stunning brunette entered the show in 2020 - when she had just 7,000 Instagram followers.

Since then she has accumulated 350,000 followers across Insta and Twitter.

And her sizzling snaps has led to famous names sliding into her DMs, which allegedly includes England footballer Jack Grealish.

Her surging popularity also means she is now able to earn a living by flogging “fully uncensored” photos on OnlyFans.

Here, we take a glimpse inside Natalia’s life from her childhood celeb crushes to her own journey to becoming famous.

Who is Natalia Zoppa?

Natalia was an events manager from Manchester and was just 20 when she joined Love Island.

Before hitting the big screen she described herself as “energetic and adventurous” and her celeb crushes included Dwayne Johnson and Justin Bieber.

She said some of her best features were her smile and laugh and she scored herself a generous 8.5 out of ten when it came to her appearance.

Love Island

The brunette beauty entered the 2020 Love Island season as a latecomer.

And she made an instant impact when Bieber lookalike Luke Mabbott brought her back from Casa Amor.

But she ruthlessly ditched him once they returned to the main villa before she coupled up with Jamie Clayton.

Jamie and Natalia were dumped from the villa in the final week of the show and their brief romance never lasted.

Luke meanwhile is now engaged to former Love Islander Lucy Donlan.

OnlyFans

Many Love Islanders leave the dating show and team up with big fashion brands or end up working in TV full-time.

But there are others who have joined OnlyFans instead – an adult subscription site where people pay a monthly fee for exclusive content.

The ex-islanders who turned to the site include Tom Walker, Arabella Chi, Adam Collard, Megan Barton Hanson, India Reynolds, Harley Judge, Katie Salmon, Hannah Elizabeth, Josh Ritchie and Thomas Powell.

And also on this list is Natalia Zoppa.

Her enticing OnlyFans bio reads: “From Love Island to OnlyFans. This is my naughtiest page.

“Fully uncensored content uploaded daily. Solo and couple content. Celebs and all your favourite Instagram girls naked.”

Natalia also teases that she allows fans to have one to one chats with her.

She charges approximately £11 per month for access to the page and she has previously boasted about teaming up with reality star twins Leonie and Che McSorley.

Natalia wrote on Instagram: “I’m in a McSorely sandwich. Check my bio to see more.”

Jack Grealish

Natalia’s seductive snaps allegedly caught the eye of Jack Grealish last year too.

According to reports, Grealish sent the Love Islander a waving hand emoji on Instagram DMs.

But Natalia’s boyfriend, Hass Saleh, posted a video on TikTok.

In it he said: “As if today couldn’t get any weirder, the best player in the Premier League tried to chat up my missus.”

He then pointed the camera at Natalia and joked: “Send him the OnlyFans link - he'll be a good tipper!"

But Natalia laughed back and said: “I’m not going to send him the OnlyFans link.”

At the time The Sun’s source said: “It was all a bit of a laugh for Natalia and her boyfriend. Neither of them took it seriously.

“Hass is not surprised Jack would be attracted to her because she’s gorgeous and he’s proud of her.

“He wasn’t having a go at Jack, he thought it was a good opportunity for her to draw attention to her social media and OnlyFans because she thrives on having as many followers as possible.”
