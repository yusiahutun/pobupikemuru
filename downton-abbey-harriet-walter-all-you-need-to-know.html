    Who is Harriet Walter?

    Harriet Walter is a highly acclaimed British actress who has been in the industry for over 40 years. She began her professional career working on the stage in many famed productions courtesy of the Royal Shakespeare Company including and , before being made associate artist at the company in 1987. Harriet also appeared in RSC productions on Broadway. Harriet's partner was actor Peter Blythe before his sad passing in 2004. Since 2011, she has married to Guy Schuessler.
