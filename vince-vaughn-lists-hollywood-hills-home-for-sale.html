Vince Vaughn lists Hollywood Hills home for 2.1million: see photos

Publish date: 2024-05-29

Vince Vaughn is selling his home in the Hollywood Hills for $2.69million (around £2.08million). The Wedding Crashers star bought the property from Kate Bosworth in 2014 for $2.375million (around £1.84million), according to property website Trulia.

RELATED: See more celebrity homes here

Located in the celebrity hotspot of the Hollywood Hills, it is easy to see why both Vince and Kate were charmed by the property. The spacious home offers peace and privacy, and is set back on a half-acre estate behind a discreet gated driveway, but is still conveniently located within Los Angeles.

Vince's contemporary home has 2,890 square feet of space, with three bedrooms and three bathrooms, and multiple outdoor areas complete with a lap pool and al fresco living room. The property has a spacious open plan layout and modern décor, with incredible canyon views from its terrace.
