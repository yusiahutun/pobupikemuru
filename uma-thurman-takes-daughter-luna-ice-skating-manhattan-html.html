Uma Thurman takes daughter Luna ice skating in Manhattan

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Uma Thurman spent her Saturday taking her four-year-old daughter Luna out for a bit of ice skating in Manhattan's Meatpacking District.

The actress was also spotted enjoying a cigarette, as well as having a chat with a scruffy-looking man in a charcoal leather jacket.

A day before, she'd given testimony at Manhattan Supreme Court as part of the rancorous custody battle she's locked in with Luna's father Arpad Busson.

A day out: Uma Thurman spent her Saturday taking her four-year-old daughter Altalune 'Luna' out for a bit of ice skating in Manhattan's Meatpacking District

During Saturday's outing, she wore a stylish pair of tight off-white trousers, wrapping herself in a black and white scarf.

Her white top's left cuff was folded back over her slimming pine green coat, and when she'd not got skates on, the 46-year-old wore faded olive green boots.

Whilst at the stand on Friday, she accused the French financier of having 'a very aggressive tantrum in front of all the kids' in June 2009.

Smoke break: At one point, she was spotted enjoying a cigarette outdoors as well

Per her account, the alleged outburst took place after Thurman called off a holiday to the Bahamas with Busson because her elder daughter Maya had broken her arms.

'He told his sons to get up and go out to dinner with him,' the film star said, telling the court that 'It was very traumatic.'

Part of the testimony she delivered on Friday detailed her relationship history with the 53-year-old Boulogne-Billancourt native, whom she'd never married.

Walk 'n' talk: She'd also been seen having a chat with a scruffy-looking man in a charcoal leather jacket

Having met in Rome in 2007, they affianced in 2008, but 'Things worsened over the course of the year,' during which 'He became stressed, very angry, very difficult to be with and he became meaner.'

Describing the 'terrible effect on his business' the 2008 financial crisis and Bernard Madoff's infamous Ponzi scheme had, she recalled that they broke their engagement during the autumn of 2009. 

Not long after, they were back on. When, in 2011, she told him she was expecting Luna, 'He looked at me and said: "Well the child will not share your last name, will not be a U.S. citizen, will not have a U.S. passport and will be raised a Catholic."'

New York chic: The Kill Bill star wore a stylish pair of tight off-white trousers, wrapping herself in a black and white scarf

Under cross-examination by Busson's lawyer, she discussed a November 2015 weekend when she'd been to St Barts and Luna'd fallen down, requiring stitches.

Whilst Busson told the court on Thursday that Thurman had left the little girl with nannies, Thurman's said that her mother and son Levon had been caring for Luna over the course of that weekend.

Thurman was also questioned about her romance with hotelier André Balazs. On Thursday, Busson alleged she'd been at the beach with him on the French Antillean island at the time when Luna'd had her stitches applied to one eye.

The actress, however, insisted in court that ‘I was visiting with friends, I was not, whatever he thought,' saying at another point that Balazs hadn't been 'on that trip,' but had been at St Barts with her 'At other times.'

On Thursday, Thurman's lawyer questioned Busson about allegations he'd been 'addicted to prostitues' and had once threatened his ex-partner Elle Macpherson.

Busson's lawyer Peter Bronstein objected to both lines of inquiry, and Busson himself denied both accusations.

Maya, who's now 18, and Levon, who's now 15, are the results of the Kill Bill star's marriage to Ethan Hawke, which lasted from 1998 until 2005.

Meanwhile, Busson's relationship with Macpherson produced an 18-year-old son also called Arpad, as well as a 13-year-old son called Aurelius.
